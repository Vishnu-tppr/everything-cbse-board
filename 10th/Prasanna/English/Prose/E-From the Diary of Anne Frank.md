# E-From the Diary of Anne Frank

> **First Flight — Ch 4** | Author: **Anne Frank** | [[📖 English]]

## Theme
Adolescence, loneliness, self-expression through writing

## Summary
Anne Frank, a 13-year-old Jewish girl hiding from Nazis in Amsterdam, writes in her diary (Kitty). She feels that paper is more patient than people. She describes her life, school, teachers (especially Mr. Keesing), and her feelings of isolation.

## Key Points
- Anne treats her diary as her best friend "Kitty"
- "Paper has more patience than people"
- Mr. Keesing punishes her for talking → she writes essays creatively
- Historical context: WWII, Holocaust, Nazi persecution

## Exam Q Patterns
- Extract: Anne's reflections on loneliness
- SA: Why did Anne think she could confide in her diary?
- SA: How did Anne handle Mr. Keesing's punishments?
