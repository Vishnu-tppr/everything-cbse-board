# E-Glimpses of India

> **First Flight — Ch 5** | Three sub-stories | [[📖 English]]

## Part I: A Baker from Goa — Lucio Rodrigues
- Portuguese influence on Goan culture
- Baker (pader) — important figure in Goan village life
- Bread: bol, pão, sweet bread for festivals
- Baker's furnace = prosperity marker

## Part II: Coorg — Lokesh Abrol
- Coorg (Kodagu): smallest district of Karnataka
- People: possibly Greek/Arab descent, martial traditions
- Coffee, cardamom, pepper plantations
- Adventure: river rafting, trekking, wildlife

## Part III: Tea from Assam — Arup Kumar Datta
- Assam: largest tea-producing region
- Legend: Bodhidharma's eyelids → tea plants
- Chinese legend: emperor's cup, tea leaves fell in
- Pranjol and Rajvir's train journey through tea gardens
