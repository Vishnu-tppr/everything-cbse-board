# E-The Proposal

> **First Flight — Ch 9** | Author: **Anton Chekhov** | Drama/Play | [[📖 English]]

## Theme
Satire on marriage, pettiness of landed gentry, absurdity of arguments

## Summary
Lomov comes to propose to Natalya, his neighbor. Before he can propose, they argue over who owns Oxen Meadows. Lomov leaves. Natalya's father Chubukov reveals the purpose of the visit — she calls Lomov back. They argue again about whose dog is better. Despite constant fighting, they get engaged.

## Key Points
- **Lomov**: nervous, hypochondriac, 35 years old
- **Natalya**: strong-willed, argumentative
- **Chubukov**: Natalya's father, also argumentative
- Irony: proposal never actually happens properly
- Satire: trivial arguments over Oxen Meadows and dogs
