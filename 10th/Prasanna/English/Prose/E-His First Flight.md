# E-His First Flight

> **First Flight — Ch 3 (Part a)** | Author: **Liam O'Flaherty** | [[📖 English]]

## Theme
Overcoming fear, self-belief, taking the first step

## Summary
A young seagull is afraid to fly. His family encourages and then abandons him on the ledge. Driven by hunger, he dives for fish his mother holds out — and discovers he can fly. The family celebrates as he joins them.

## Key Points
- Fear paralyzes; hunger motivates
- Family's tough love forces the seagull to act
- The first step is the hardest
- **Lesson**: You'll never know until you try

## Exam Q Patterns
- Extract: young seagull's feelings before the flight
- SA: How did the seagull's family motivate him?
- Value: What lesson does the story teach about fear?
