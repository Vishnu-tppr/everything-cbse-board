# E-Black Aeroplane

> **First Flight — Ch 3 (Part b)** | Author: **Frederick Forsyth** | [[📖 English]]

## Theme
Mystery, unknown help, gratitude

## Summary
A pilot flying to England encounters a massive storm. A mysterious black aeroplane appears and guides him to safety. When he lands and asks about the other plane, the woman at the control tower says there was no other plane on the radar.

## Key Points
- Storm: cumulonimbus clouds, no fuel, instruments dead
- Black aeroplane with no lights guided him
- **Mystery**: who was the pilot? No record exists
- Open-ended story — possibly imagination, guardian angel, or supernatural

## Exam Q Patterns
- Extract: pilot's feelings in the storm
- SA: Describe the mysterious experience of the pilot
- SA: Why was the pilot shocked at the airfield?
