# E-Madam Rides the Bus

> **First Flight — Ch 7** | Author: **Valliammai Subramanian** | [[📖 English]]

## Theme
Curiosity, growing up, first independent experience, understanding of life and death

## Summary
Valli, an 8-year-old Tamil girl, plans and takes a solo bus ride to the nearest town. She observes everything with curiosity and joy. On the return trip, she sees a dead cow on the road — the same one she saw happily running alongside the bus earlier. This experience gives her a deeper understanding of life.

## Key Points
- Valli saves money (6 paise each way) by sacrificing peppermints/toys
- She plans meticulously by observing bus timings
- Joy of independence: refuses to be treated as a child
- Dead cow: first encounter with death → sobers her mood
- Returns home keeping the adventure secret from her mother

## Exam Q Patterns
- Extract: Valli's observations on the bus
- SA: How did Valli plan her bus ride?
- SA: What change did Valli undergo during the return journey?
