# E-Mijbil the Otter

> **First Flight — Ch 6** | Author: **Gavin Maxwell** | [[📖 English]]

## Theme
Human-animal bond, companionship, adapting to new environments

## Summary
Gavin Maxwell buys an otter (Mijbil) in Iraq and takes it to London. The otter loves water, invents games, and becomes his close companion. At first, people don't recognize it — some think it's a baby seal or teddy bear.

## Key Points
- Maxwell gets Mijbil from the Tigris marshes
- Otter's first bath: transformation from apathy to joy
- Invented a ball game on the staircase
- Flight to London: otter escapes from box → chaos on plane
- New species named after Maxwell: *Lutrogale perspicillata maxwelli*

## Exam Q Patterns
- Extract: Mijbil's behavior with water
- SA: How did Mijbil behave during the flight?
- SA: Why does the author say otter was "a find"?
