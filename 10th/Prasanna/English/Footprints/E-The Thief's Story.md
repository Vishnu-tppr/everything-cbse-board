# E-The Thief's Story

> **Footprints Without Feet — Ch 2** | Author: **Ruskin Bond** | [[📖 English]]

## Theme
Trust, compassion, and education can reform a criminal

## Summary
Hari Singh, a 15-year-old thief, befriends Anil, a struggling writer. Anil brings Hari home, teaches him to cook, write his name, and trust him. One night, Anil makes money; Hari steals it and runs to the station. But his conscience and the thought of losing his education stop him. He returns the money. Anil knows but says nothing, and pays Hari regularly thereafter.

## Key Points
- **Hari Singh**: experienced thief, changes his name every month, values education deep down
- **Anil**: careless with money, trusting, compassionate, forgiving
- Turning point: Hari realizes that stealing is easy, but being a truly big, clever, and respected man requires education.

## Exam Q Patterns
- SA: Why did Hari Singh return to Anil?
- SA: How did Anil's trusting nature affect Hari Singh?
- LA: "Love and sympathy can transform a thief." Discuss with reference to the story.
