# E-A Triumph of Surgery

> **Footprints Without Feet — Ch 1** | Author: **James Herriot** | [[📖 English]]

## Theme
Blind love and over-pampering vs strict discipline and proper care

## Summary
Mrs. Pumphrey, a rich lady, overfeeds her pet dog Tricki, making him hugely fat and lazy. He falls ill. James Herriot, a veterinary surgeon, takes Tricki to his surgery. He gives Tricki no medical treatment — just plenty of water, exercise, and a strict diet with other dogs. Tricki recovers completely. Mrs. Pumphrey thinks it's a "triumph of surgery".

## Key Points
- **Mrs. Pumphrey**: overly affectionate, rich, foolishly indulgent
- **Tricki**: greedy for food, becomes like a "bloated sausage"
- **James Herriot**: practical, sensible, understands the real problem
- Irony: There was no surgery performed at all.

## Exam Q Patterns
- SA: Why was Dr. Herriot worried about Tricki?
- SA: How did Dr. Herriot treat Tricki?
- LA: Describe Mrs. Pumphrey's character. Was she a good pet owner?
