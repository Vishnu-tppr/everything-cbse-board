# E-The Necklace

> **Footprints Without Feet — Ch 7** | Author: **Guy de Maupassant** | [[📖 English]]

## Theme
Contentment vs Greed, false pride, living beyond one's means

## Summary
Matilda Loisel is pretty but born into a poor clerk's family. She dreams of luxury. Invited to a grand ball, she borrows a diamond necklace from her rich friend, Mme Forestier. She loses it. Instead of telling the truth, she and her husband borrow 36,000 francs to buy a replacement. They spend 10 years in extreme poverty repaying the debt, destroying her beauty. Years later, she meets Mme Forestier, only to learn the original necklace was fake, worth only 500 francs.

## Key Points
- **Matilda**: dissatisfied, ambitious, vain, ruins her life due to false pride.
- **Mr. Loisel**: loving, practical husband who sacrifices his savings and 10 years of his life for her mistake.
- **The Twist**: A single moment of honesty would have saved them 10 years of misery.

## Exam Q Patterns
- SA: Why was Matilda unhappy with her life?
- SA: How did Mr. Loisel contribute to the cost of the new necklace?
- LA: The course of the Loisels' life changed due to the necklace. Comment. / Honesty is the best policy.
