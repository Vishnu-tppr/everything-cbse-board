# E-Fog

> **Poet**: Carl Sandburg | [[📖 English]]

## Theme
Nature's silent, mysterious ways

## Key Points
- Very short poem (6 lines)
- Fog comes silently "on little cat feet"
- It sits looking over the harbor and city on "silent haunches"
- Then it simply moves on

## Literary Devices
- Extended Metaphor / Personification: Fog is compared to a cat
- Free verse: No rhyme or regular meter

## Exam Q Patterns
- Extract: How does the fog come?
- SA: Why does the poet compare the fog to a cat? Provide 3 reasons.
  (Answers: comes silently, sits on haunches watching, moves away quietly)
