# E-For Anne Gregory

> **Poet**: W.B. Yeats | [[📖 English]]

## Theme
Inner beauty vs Outer beauty, true love

## Key Points
- A young man might fall in despair because of Anne's beautiful "yellow hair"
- He loves her for her hair, not for "herself alone"
- Anne replies she can dye her hair brown, black, or carrot so men will love her for herself, not her hair
- The speaker says he heard a religious man declare that only God can love you for yourself alone, and not your yellow hair

## Literary Devices
- Conversational tone / Dialogue format
- Metaphor: "great honey-coloured ramparts at your ear" (hair compared to fort walls protecting her face)

## Exam Q Patterns
- Extract: What does Anne want to do with her hair and why?
- SA: What truth did the religious man declare?
- Value: What is the poet trying to say about human love vs divine love?
