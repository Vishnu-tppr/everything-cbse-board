# E-The Trees

> **Poet**: Adrienne Rich | [[📖 English]]

## Theme
Nature reclaiming its space, freedom, feminism

## Key Points
- Trees that were kept inside houses (as decorative plants) are breaking out
- Roots work all night to free themselves from cracks in the veranda floor
- Leaves strain toward the glass; twigs are stiff
- Like newly discharged patients, they move half-dazed to the clinic doors
- The poet sits writing letters, not mentioning the departure
- The forest, empty all these days, will be full of trees by morning
- The moon is broken like a mirror by the oak tree

## Literary Devices
- Personification: Trees are described like patients
- Simile: "like newly discharged patients"
- Symbolism: Trees breaking out = women breaking out of domestic boundaries / nature breaking free of human control

## Exam Q Patterns
- Extract: What are the roots and leaves doing?
- SA: Explain the "newly discharged patients" comparison
- LA: What is the deeper, symbolic meaning of the poem?
