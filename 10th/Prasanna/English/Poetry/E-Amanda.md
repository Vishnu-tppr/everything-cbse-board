# E-Amanda

> **Poet**: Robin Klein | [[📖 English]]

## Theme
Parental control vs child's desire for freedom and imagination

## Key Points
- Mother constantly nags Amanda: don't bite nails, sit straight, finish homework, clean shoes, don't eat chocolate (acne)
- Amanda escapes into her imagination (given in brackets):
  - **Mermaid**: drifting blissfully in a languid emerald sea
  - **Orphan**: roaming streets, making patterns in dust with bare feet
  - **Rapunzel**: living peacefully in a tower, never letting her hair down
- In the end, the mother tells her to stop sulking so people won't think she's being harassed

## Literary Devices
- Contrast: Real world (nagging) vs Imaginary world (peace)
- Allusion: Rapunzel, Mermaid
- Repetition: "Amanda!"

## Exam Q Patterns
- Extract: Identify who is speaking to Amanda
- SA: Why does Amanda want to be an orphan or Rapunzel?
- LA: Discuss the parenting style shown in the poem.
