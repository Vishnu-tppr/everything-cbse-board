# E-How to Tell Wild Animals

> **Poet**: Carolyn Wells | [[📖 English]]

## Theme
Humorous and dangerous ways to identify wild animals

## Key Points
- **Asian Lion**: Roars as you're dying
- **Bengal Tiger**: Yellow background, black stripes, eats you
- **Leopard**: Peppered with spots, leaps on you
- **Bear**: Hugs you very hard
- **Hyena vs Crocodile**: Hyena comes with merry smiles, crocodile comes with tears
- **Chameleon**: Looks like a lizard, no ears or wings; if there's nothing on the tree, it's a chameleon

## Literary Devices
- Humour / Irony: Suggesting you'll know the animal when it kills you
- Rhyme scheme: ababcc

## Exam Q Patterns
- Extract: Identify the animal being described
- SA: How does the poet distinguish a hyena from a crocodile?
- SA: What is the central idea of the poem?
