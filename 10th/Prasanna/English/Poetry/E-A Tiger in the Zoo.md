# E-A Tiger in the Zoo

> **Poet**: Leslie Norris | [[📖 English]]

## Theme
Captivity vs Freedom, cruelty of keeping wild animals in small enclosures

## Key Points
- **In the zoo**: Tiger paces in a small concrete cell, ignores visitors, feels helpless anger ("quiet rage")
- **In the wild**: Tiger would be hiding in long grass, hunting deer near the water hole, terrorizing the village
- At night in the zoo, he stares at the brilliant stars with his brilliant eyes, longing for freedom

## Literary Devices
- Personification: "He stalks", "his quiet rage"
- Metaphor: "pads of velvet quiet"
- Contrast: The poem constantly shifts between the zoo cell and the wild jungle

## Exam Q Patterns
- Extract: Contrast the tiger's behavior in the zoo vs wild
- SA: Why does the tiger ignore the visitors?
- SA: Explain "quiet rage"
