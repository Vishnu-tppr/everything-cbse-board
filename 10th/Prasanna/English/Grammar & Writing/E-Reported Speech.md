# E-Reported Speech

> **Grammar** | **Marks**: Part of 10 | [[📖 English]]

## Rule 1: Tense Changes (if reporting verb is in past, e.g., 'said')
- Simple Present → Simple Past (*writes → wrote*)
- Present Continuous → Past Continuous (*is writing → was writing*)
- Present Perfect → Past Perfect (*has written → had written*)
- Simple Past → Past Perfect (*wrote → had written*)
- Will → Would
- Can → Could
- May → Might
- Must → Had to

*Exception: Universal truths do NOT change tense. (He said, "The earth is round" → He said that the earth is round.)*

## Rule 2: Pronoun Changes (SON rule)
- **1st Person (I, We)** changes according to **Subject**
- **2nd Person (You)** changes according to **Object**
- **3rd Person (He, She, It, They)** = **No change**

## Rule 3: Time and Place Word Changes
- This → That
- These → Those
- Here → There
- Now → Then
- Today → That day
- Yesterday → The previous day / the day before
- Tomorrow → The next day / the following day

## Rule 4: Types of Sentences
1. **Statements**: Use "that" connector.
2. **Questions (Wh-)**: No connector. Do not use 'if'. (He asked where I lived.)
3. **Questions (Yes/No)**: Use "if" or "whether".
4. **Orders/Requests**: Use "to" + V1. (He requested me to help him.)

## Exam Traps ⚠️
- Converting a question to reported speech: The word order must change to Statement form!
  - ❌ He asked where was I going.
  - ✅ He asked where I was going.
