# E-Analytical Paragraph

> **Writing Skills** | **Marks**: 5 | [[📖 English]]

## What is it?
Writing a paragraph of 100-120 words analyzing a given chart, graph, table, map, or cue. 

## Structure (3 Parts)

1. **Introduction (1-2 sentences)**
   - What does the chart show? 
   - *Example: The given bar graph illustrates the percentage of students using different modes of transport to school in the year 2023.*

2. **Body (Details & Comparisons) (4-6 sentences)**
   - Do NOT just list the data. **Compare** and **contrast**.
   - Group similar trends together (e.g., highest and lowest).
   - *Phrases: "A striking feature is...", "In stark contrast to...", "Accounts for the majority...", "Shows a steep decline..."*

3. **Conclusion (1-2 sentences)**
   - Overall summary or trend (Do not give your personal opinion unless asked).
   - *Example: Overall, it is evident that public transport remains the most preferred choice, while walking is the least popular.*

## Exam Traps ⚠️
- Giving personal opinions or adding external information not in the chart.
- Just writing numbers as a list instead of comparing them.
- Writing more than 1 paragraph (it must be ONE analytical block).
