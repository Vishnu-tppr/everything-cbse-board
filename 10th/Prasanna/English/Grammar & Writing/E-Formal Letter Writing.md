# E-Formal Letter Writing

> **Writing Skills** | **Marks**: 5 | [[📖 English]]

## Types of Formal Letters
1. Letter to the Editor (Highlighting an issue)
2. Letter of Inquiry (Asking for details/brochure)
3. Letter of Complaint (Defective item/poor service)
4. Placing an Order (School equipment/books)

## Perfect Format (1 Mark)
```text
[Sender's Address]
123, ABC Colony
New Delhi

[Date]
14 March 2024

[Receiver's Designation & Address]
The Editor
The Times of India
New Delhi

[Subject]
Subject: [Brief, clear, 4-6 words]

[Salutation]
Respected Sir / Madam,

[Body - 3 Paragraphs]
Para 1: Introduction / Purpose of writing (Through the columns of your esteemed newspaper...)
Para 2: Details of the problem / Inquiry / Complaint
Para 3: Conclusion / Action required / Suggestion

[Complimentary Close]
Yours faithfully,
[Signature]
[Name in block letters]
```

## Useful Phrases for "Letter to the Editor"
- **Opening**: "Through the columns of your esteemed newspaper, I wish to draw the attention of the concerned authorities towards..."
- **Closing**: "I hope this letter finds a place in your newspaper so the authorities can wake up and take necessary action."

## Exam Traps ⚠️
- Writing date as 14/03/24 ❌ Use 14 March 2024 ✅
- Mixing Yours and faithfully (Yours' faithfully ❌ Yours faithfully ✅)
- Being informal or using slang.
