# Keywords Bank (SST & English)

> **Quick Reference Guide** | Enhance your answer quality | [[🌍 Social Science]] | [[📖 English]]

## History Keywords
- **Nationalism**: A strong feeling of pride and devotion to one's country.
- **Satyagraha**: Non-violent resistance; truth-force (Gandhi's philosophy).
- **Boycott**: Refusal to buy or use goods as a form of protest.
- **Purna Swaraj**: Complete independence (demanded at Lahore Session, 1929).
- **Proto-industrialisation**: Large scale industrial production for international market before the factory system.
- **Indentured Labour**: Bonded laborer under contract to work for an employer for a specific time.

## Geography Keywords
- **Resource**: Everything available in our environment which can be used to satisfy our needs, provided it is technologically accessible, economically feasible, and culturally acceptable.
- **Sustainable Development**: Economic development without damaging the environment and compromising the needs of future generations.
- **Multipurpose Project**: A dam or river valley project serving multiple purposes (irrigation, electricity, flood control).
- **Kharif / Rabi / Zaid**: The three distinct cropping seasons of India.
- **Golden Quadrilateral**: Super highway network connecting Delhi, Mumbai, Chennai, and Kolkata.

## Political Science Keywords
- **Federalism**: System of government where power is divided between a central authority and constituent units.
- **Decentralization**: Taking power away from Central and State governments and giving it to local government.
- **Majoritarianism**: A belief that the majority community should be able to rule a country in whichever way it wants.
- **Secular State**: A state that does not establish any one religion as the official religion.
- **Coalition Government**: A government formed by the coming together of at least two political parties.

## Economics Keywords
- **Per Capita Income**: Total income of the area divided by total population (Average Income).
- **GDP (Gross Domestic Product)**: Total value of all final goods and services produced within a country in a year.
- **Collateral**: An asset that the borrower owns and uses as a guarantee to a lender until the loan is repaid.
- **Globalisation**: The process of rapid integration or interconnection between countries.
- **Organized Sector**: Enterprises where the terms of employment are regular and follow government rules.
