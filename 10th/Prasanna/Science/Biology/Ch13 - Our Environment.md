# Ch13 — Our Environment

> **Biology** | **Marks**: 5 | [[🔬 Science]]

## Key Concepts

- **Ecosystem**: biotic + abiotic interacting
- **Trophic levels**: Producers (T1) → Primary consumers (T2) → Secondary (T3) → Tertiary (T4)
- **10% Law**: Only 10% energy transfers to next level

```
10% LAW EXAMPLE:
  T1 Producers:     10,000 J
  T2 Herbivores:     1,000 J
  T3 Carnivores:       100 J
  T4 Top carnivores:    10 J
```

## Ozone

```
FORMATION: O₂ →UV→ O + O, then O + O₂ → O₃
DEPLETION: CFCs →UV→ Cl, then Cl + O₃ → ClO + O₂
           (Cl acts as catalyst, one Cl destroys many O₃)
```

## Biodegradable vs Non-biodegradable

| Biodegradable | Non-biodegradable |
|--------------|-------------------|
| Paper, food waste | Plastic, glass, metal |
| Broken down by microorganisms | NOT broken down |
| Compostable | Must recycle/dispose |
