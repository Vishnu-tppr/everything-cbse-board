# Ch12 — Magnetic Effects of Electric Current

> **Physics** | **Marks**: Part of 13 | [[🔬 Science]]

## Key Rules

```
RIGHT-HAND THUMB RULE:
  Thumb → direction of current
  Fingers (curled) → direction of magnetic field

FLEMING'S LEFT HAND RULE (Motor):
  Forefinger → magnetic Field
  Middle finger → Current
  Thumb → Force/Motion (thrust)

FLEMING'S RIGHT HAND RULE (Generator):
  Thumb → Motion of conductor
  Forefinger → magnetic Field
  Middle finger → induced Current
```

## Key Devices

```
ELECTRIC MOTOR: Electrical → Mechanical energy
  Uses: Fleming's LEFT Hand Rule
  Parts: Coil, magnets, split ring commutator, brushes, axle

ELECTRIC GENERATOR: Mechanical → Electrical energy
  Uses: Electromagnetic induction (Fleming's RIGHT Hand Rule)
  AC Generator: slip rings → alternating current
  DC Generator: split ring commutator → direct current
```

## Domestic Circuits

```
Supply: 220V AC, 50Hz
Fuse: thin wire in LIVE wire, melts on overload
Three wires: LIVE (red), NEUTRAL (black), EARTH (green)
Short circuit: live touches neutral → high current → fuse melts
```

## Common Exam Traps ⚠️

1. Confusing Left Hand (motor) with Right Hand (generator)
2. AC generator uses slip rings, DC uses split rings
3. Not labeling all parts of motor/generator diagrams
