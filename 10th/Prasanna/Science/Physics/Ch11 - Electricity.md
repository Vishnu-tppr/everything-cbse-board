# Ch11 — Electricity ⭐

> **Physics** | **Marks**: Part of 13 | [[🔬 Science]]
> ⭐ **HIGH YIELD** — 5M LA almost guaranteed (Ohm's law + series/parallel)

## Key Formulas

```
CURRENT: I = Q/t (A = C/s)
POTENTIAL DIFFERENCE: V = W/Q (V = J/C)
OHM'S LAW: V = IR
RESISTANCE: R = V/I (Ω)
RESISTIVITY: R = ρl/A (ρ in Ω·m)

SERIES:    R_total = R₁ + R₂ + R₃    (same current, V splits)
PARALLEL:  1/R_total = 1/R₁ + 1/R₂ + 1/R₃  (same voltage, I splits)

JOULE'S LAW: H = I²Rt = VIt = V²t/R
POWER: P = VI = I²R = V²/R (Watt)
ENERGY: E = Pt = VIt (Joule)
1 kWh = 3.6 × 10⁶ J
```

## Circuit Symbols

```
—/\/\/\— Resistor      —|⊕|— Ammeter (SERIES)
—||— Cell               —⊗— Voltmeter (PARALLEL)
—|  |— Battery          ○/ ○ Switch (open)
```

## Common Exam Traps ⚠️

1. Ammeter in SERIES, Voltmeter in PARALLEL — don't mix
2. In parallel: total resistance < smallest individual resistance
3. kWh to Joule conversion errors
4. Not drawing circuit diagrams with proper symbols
