> **Physics** | **Marks**: Part of 12 | [[🔬 Science]]

## Key Concepts

**Reflection:** Laws of reflection, concave/convex mirror properties, mirror formula
**Refraction:** Snell's Law, convex/concave lens, lens formula, power of lens

## Key Formulas

```
MIRROR: 1/v + 1/u = 1/f     m = -v/u = h'/h
LENS:   1/v - 1/u = 1/f     m = v/u = h'/h
POWER:  P = 1/f (f in metres) → unit: Dioptre (D)
REFRACTIVE INDEX: n = sin i / sin r = c/v
COMBINED POWER: P = P₁ + P₂ + P₃

Sign Convention (New Cartesian):
  Object always on left → u is always NEGATIVE
  Concave mirror/lens: f is negative / positive
```

## Image Formation — Concave Mirror

| Object | Image | Nature | Size |
|--------|-------|--------|------|
| At infinity | At F | Real, inverted | Highly diminished |
| Beyond C | Between F and C | Real, inverted | Diminished |
| At C | At C | Real, inverted | Same size |
| Between C and F | Beyond C | Real, inverted | Enlarged |
| At F | At infinity | Real, inverted | Highly enlarged |
| Between F and P | Behind mirror | Virtual, erect | Enlarged |

## Common Exam Traps ⚠️

1. **Sign convention errors** — u is ALWAYS negative
2. Confusing mirror magnification (-v/u) with lens (v/u)
3. Power: forgetting to convert f to METRES before calculating
4. Not drawing at least 2 rays in ray diagrams
