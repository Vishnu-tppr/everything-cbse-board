# Ch10 — The Human Eye and the Colourful World

> **Physics** | **Marks**: 2-3 | [[🔬 Science]]

## Key Concepts

- Eye structure: cornea, iris, pupil, lens, retina, optic nerve
- Power of accommodation
- **Myopia** (near-sighted) → corrected by **concave** lens
- **Hypermetropia** (far-sighted) → corrected by **convex** lens
- **Presbyopia** → corrected by **bifocal** lens
- Atmospheric refraction: twinkling of stars, advanced sunrise
- Dispersion: VIBGYOR (violet shortest λ, red longest λ)
- Scattering: Tyndall effect, blue sky, red sunset

## Key Formulas

```
MYOPIA: f = -far point distance (concave lens)
HYPERMETROPIA: f = near point distance (convex lens)
VIBGYOR: Violet > Indigo > Blue > Green > Yellow > Orange > Red
  Violet: shortest wavelength, max deviation
  Red: longest wavelength, min deviation
```

## Common Exam Traps ⚠️

1. Confusing myopia (concave) and hypermetropia (convex)
2. Not explaining WHY stars twinkle (changing atmospheric density)
3. Forgetting Tyndall effect as cause of scattering
