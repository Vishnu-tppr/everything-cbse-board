> **Unit IV: Geometry** | **Marks**: 5-8 (every paper) | [[📘 Mathematics]]
> ⭐ **HIGH YIELD** — 5M proof guaranteed

## Key Concepts

- **BPT (Basic Proportionality Theorem / Thales)**: line ∥ to one side divides other two proportionally
- Converse of BPT
- **Similarity criteria**: AAA, AA, SSS, SAS
- **Area theorem**: ratio of areas = square of ratio of sides
- **Pythagoras Theorem** and its converse

## Key Formulas

```
If △ABC ~ △DEF:
  AB/DE = BC/EF = CA/FD
  Area(△ABC)/Area(△DEF) = (AB/DE)² = (BC/EF)² = (CA/FD)²

Pythagoras: hypotenuse² = base² + perpendicular²
```

## Common Exam Traps ⚠️

1. Not writing correct order of similarity (△ABC ~ △DEF means A↔D, B↔E, C↔F)
2. Forgetting to prove triangles are similar BEFORE using ratios
3. Not mentioning the theorem name in proofs
4. Missing "Hence proved" at the end
