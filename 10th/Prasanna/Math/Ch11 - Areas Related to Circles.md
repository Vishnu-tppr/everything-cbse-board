# Ch11 — Areas Related to Circles

> **Unit VI: Mensuration** | **Marks**: Part of 10 | [[📘 Mathematics]]

## Key Formulas

```
Area of circle = πr²
Circumference = 2πr
Area of sector = (θ/360°) × πr²
Length of arc = (θ/360°) × 2πr
Area of segment = Area of sector - Area of triangle
Area of ring = π(R² - r²)
```

## Common Exam Traps ⚠️

1. Using degrees vs radians confusion
2. Forgetting area of segment formula
3. Not using π = 22/7 when specified
