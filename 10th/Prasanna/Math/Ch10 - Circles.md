# Ch10 — Circles

> **Unit IV: Geometry** | **Marks**: Part of 15 | [[📘 Mathematics]]

## Key Concepts

- Tangent ⊥ Radius at point of contact
- From external point: exactly 2 tangents, equal in length (PA = PB)
- ∠OAP = 90° (OA = radius, PA = tangent)

## Key Formulas

```
Tangent ⊥ Radius at point of contact
PA = PB (tangents from external point P)
∠OAP = 90°
```

## Common Exam Traps ⚠️

1. Not mentioning OA ⊥ PA in proofs
2. Forgetting tangent lengths are equal
3. Not drawing proper diagrams
