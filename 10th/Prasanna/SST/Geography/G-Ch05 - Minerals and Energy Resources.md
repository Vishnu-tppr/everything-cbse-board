# G-Ch05 — Minerals and Energy Resources

> **Geography** | **Marks**: 3-4 + MAP | [[🌍 Social Science]]

## Types of Minerals

| Type | Examples | Extraction |
|------|----------|-----------|
| Metallic — Ferrous | Iron ore, Manganese | Mining |
| Metallic — Non-ferrous | Copper, Bauxite, Gold | Mining |
| Non-metallic | Mica, Limestone, Salt | Mining/Quarrying |

## Iron Ore Belts

```
1. Odisha-Jharkhand belt: Hematite (Noamundi, Bonai)
2. Durg-Bastar-Chandrapur: Bailadila (Chhattisgarh)
3. Bellary-Chitradurga-Tumkur: Karnataka
4. Maharashtra-Goa: Ratnagiri
```

## Energy Resources

| Type | Examples | Map Items |
|------|----------|-----------|
| Coal | Raniganj, Jharia, Bokaro, Singrauli | WB, Jharkhand, MP |
| Petroleum | Mumbai High, Gujarat, Assam | Offshore + onshore |
| Nuclear | Rawatbhata (Rajasthan), Tarapur (MH), Kalpakkam (TN), Narora (UP), Kakrapara (Gujarat) | All states |

## Conservation

- Use renewable sources (solar, wind, hydro)
- Reduce wastage, recycle metals
- Use CNG, biogas as alternatives
