## Key Industries

| Industry | Raw Material | Location Factors | Major Centers |
|----------|-------------|-------------------|---------------|
| Iron & Steel | Iron ore, coal | Near raw materials | Jamshedpur, Bhilai, Durgapur, Rourkela |
| Cotton Textile | Cotton | Near cotton belt | Mumbai, Ahmedabad, Coimbatore |
| Sugar | Sugarcane | Near farms (perishable) | UP, Maharashtra, Karnataka |
| IT | Skilled labour | Near cities | Bangalore, Hyderabad, Chennai, Pune |

## Industrial Pollution & Control

```
AIR: smoke, gas → use scrubbers, electrostatic precipitators
WATER: chemical effluents → treatment plants before discharge
LAND: solid waste → 3Rs (Reduce, Reuse, Recycle)
NOISE: machinery → earplugs, silencers, green belts
```
