## Rainwater Harvesting

```
Traditional:
  Rajasthan: Khadins, Johads, Tankas
  Meghalaya: Bamboo drip irrigation
  Tamil Nadu: Eris (tanks)
  Karnataka: Gul, Kul systems

Modern: Rooftop → Underground tank → Recharge
```
