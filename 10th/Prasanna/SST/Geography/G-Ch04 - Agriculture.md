> **Geography** | **Marks**: 4-5 + MAP WORK | [[🌍 Social Science]]
> ⭐ **HIGH YIELD** — Crop conditions + map work

## Crop Seasons

| Season | Period | Crops |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Kharif | June-Oct (monsoon) | Rice, maize, jowar, bajra, cotton, jute |
| Rabi | Nov-Mar (winter) | Wheat, barley, gram, mustard |
| Zaid | Mar-Jun (summer) | Watermelon, muskmelon, cucumber |

## Major Crops — Conditions

| Crop | Temperature | Rainfall | Soil | Major States |
|------|-------------|----------|------|-------------|
| Rice | 25°C | >100cm | Alluvial | WB, UP, Punjab |
| Wheat | 10-15°C | 75cm | Alluvial/Black | UP, Punjab, Haryana |
| Sugarcane | 21-27°C | 75-100cm | Alluvial/Black | UP, Maharashtra, Karnataka |
| Cotton | Hot | Moderate | Black | Maharashtra, Gujarat, MP |
| Jute | 27°C | >200cm | Alluvial | WB, Bihar, Assam |
| Tea | 20-30°C | >200cm | Laterite/Loamy | Assam, WB, Kerala |
| Coffee | 15-28°C | 150-250cm | Laterite | Karnataka, Kerala, TN |
| Rubber | 25°C | >200cm | Laterite | Kerala |

## Revolutions

| Revolution | Product |
|-----------|---------|
| Green | Foodgrains (wheat, rice) |
| White | Milk (Operation Flood) |
| Yellow | Oilseeds |
| Blue | Fish |
| Golden | Horticulture/Honey |

## Common Exam Traps ⚠️

1. Confusing Kharif and Rabi crops
2. Wrong conditions for tea (needs acidic laterite soil)
3. Not knowing revolution names
