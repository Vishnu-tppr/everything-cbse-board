## Three Major Movements

```
1. NON-COOPERATION (1920-22):
   Trigger: Jallianwala Bagh (1919), Rowlatt Act
   Methods: Boycott of schools, courts, foreign cloth, elections
   Withdrawal: Chauri Chaura violence (Feb 1922)

2. CIVIL DISOBEDIENCE (1930-34):
   Trigger: Lahore Session (1929) — Purna Swaraj demand
   Start: Dandi March (12 Mar 1930) — 240 miles, 24 days
   Salt Law broken → mass arrests
   Gandhi-Irwin Pact (1931), Round Table Conferences

3. QUIT INDIA (1942):
   "Do or Die" — Gandhi
   British must leave India immediately
   Mass arrests of leaders
```
