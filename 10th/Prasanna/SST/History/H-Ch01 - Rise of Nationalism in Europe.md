# H-Ch01 — The Rise of Nationalism in Europe

> **History** | **Marks**: Part of 20 | [[🌍 Social Science]]

## Key Concepts

- **Nation-state**: sovereign territory with shared identity
- **French Revolution (1789)**: sovereignty from monarchy → people
- **Napoleonic Code (1804)**: equality before law, abolished feudalism
- **Conservatism after 1815**: Congress of Vienna, restoration of monarchies
- **Romanticism**: cultural nationalism through art, music, folklore

## Unification Movements

```
ITALY:
  Mazzini → Young Italy (revolutionary)
  Cavour → Sardinia-Piedmont (diplomatic, with France)
  Garibaldi → Southern Italy (military)
  Unified by 1861

GERMANY:
  Prussia led unification under Otto von Bismarck
  "Blood and Iron" policy
  Three wars: Danish (1864), Austrian (1866), French (1870-71)
  Kaiser William I → German Empire (1871)

GREECE: Independence from Ottoman Empire (1829)
  Treaty of Constantinople
  Support from Western Europeans (Byron)
```
