# E-Ch05 — Consumer Rights

> **Economics** | **Marks**: 3-4 | [[🌍 Social Science]]

## Consumer Rights (COPRA 2019)

| Right | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| Right to Safety | Protection from dangerous goods |
| Right to Information | Know product details (MRP, expiry, ingredients) |
| Right to Choose | Access to variety, no forced buying |
| Right to Seek Redressal | File complaint, get compensation |
| Right to Consumer Education | Know your rights |
| Right to be Heard | Consumer's voice in policymaking |

## Three-Tier Redressal System

| Forum | Claim Amount |
|-------|-------------|
| District | Up to ₹1 crore |
| State | ₹1 crore – ₹10 crore |
| National | Above ₹10 crore |

## Quality Marks

| Mark | For |
|------|-----|
| ISI | Industrial products |
| Agmark | Agricultural products |
| Hallmark | Gold jewellery |
| FSSAI | Food products |

## Key Laws

- **Consumer Protection Act (COPRA) 2019** — replaced 1986 act
- **RTI (Right to Information) 2005** — transparency in government
- **World Consumer Rights Day**: 15 March

## Common Exam Traps ⚠️

1. Confusing ISI (industrial) with Agmark (agricultural)
2. Not knowing the three-tier system amounts
3. Forgetting COPRA year (2019, NOT 1986)
