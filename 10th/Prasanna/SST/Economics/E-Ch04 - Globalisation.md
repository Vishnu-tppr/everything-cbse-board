# E-Ch04 — Globalisation and the Indian Economy

> **Economics** | **Marks**: 3-4 | [[🌍 Social Science]]

## Key Concepts

```
GLOBALISATION: Integration of economies through trade, investment, technology
MNC: Multi-National Corporation — produces in multiple countries
LIBERALISATION (1991): Reduced barriers to trade and investment
  - Removed licensing requirements
  - Reduced import duties
  - Allowed foreign investment
WTO: World Trade Organization — rules for international trade
SEZ: Special Economic Zone — tax incentives for exporters
```

## How MNCs Spread

1. Set up factories in cheap-labor countries
2. Buy local companies
3. Partner with local firms
4. Place orders with local producers (contract manufacturing)

## Impact of Globalisation

| Positive | Negative |
|----------|----------|
| More choices for consumers | Small producers suffer |
| Job creation in IT, BPO | Job losses in traditional sectors |
| Better quality products | Exploitation of workers |
| Technology transfer | Environmental concerns |
| Economic growth | Rising inequality |

## Fair Globalisation

- Government policies to protect workers
- Labor laws enforcement
- Support for small producers
- WTO reform for developing countries

## Common Exam Traps ⚠️

1. Only writing positives OR negatives (must write BOTH)
2. Not mentioning 1991 liberalisation
3. Forgetting WTO role
