# E-Ch01 — Development

> **Economics** | **Marks**: Part of 20 | [[🌍 Social Science]]

## Key Concepts

- Development = different for different people (farmer vs urban professional)
- Income alone is NOT enough → HDI (Human Development Index)
- **Per Capita Income** = Total National Income / Total Population

## Comparison of Countries

| Country | Per Capita Income | Life Expectancy | Literacy | HDI Rank |
|---------|------------------|-----------------|----------|----------|
| Sri Lanka | Low | 75 | 91% | High |
| India | Medium | 69 | 74% | Medium |
| Myanmar | Low | 67 | 83% | Low |

## Key Terms

```
BMI (Body Mass Index): weight(kg) / height²(m²)
  Undernourished: BMI < 18.5
  Normal: 18.5-24.9
  
INFANT MORTALITY RATE: deaths of infants per 1000 live births
LITERACY RATE: % of literate population (age 7+)
NET ATTENDANCE RATIO: % of children attending school
SUSTAINABILITY: meeting present needs without compromising future
```

## Common Exam Traps ⚠️

1. Using only income to define development (must include health, education)
2. Not mentioning HDI by name
3. Confusing IMR (infant mortality) with literacy rate data
