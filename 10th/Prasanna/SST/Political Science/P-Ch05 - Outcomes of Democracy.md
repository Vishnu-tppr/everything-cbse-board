# P-Ch05 — Outcomes of Democracy

> **Political Science** | **Marks**: 3-4 | [[🌍 Social Science]]

## Key Outcomes

| Dimension | What Democracy Delivers |
|-----------|------------------------|
| Accountability | Regular elections, RTI, free press |
| Responsiveness | Responds to citizen needs |
| Legitimacy | People accept government decisions |
| Economic | No guarantee of growth (but better distribution) |
| Inequality | Reduces but doesn't eliminate |
| Conflict | Democratic methods to resolve disputes |
| Dignity | Equal status, respects diversity |

## Democracy vs Dictatorship

```
DEMOCRACY IS BETTER AT:
  ✅ Promoting dignity and freedom
  ✅ Allowing peaceful resolution of conflict
  ✅ Ensuring transparency and accountability
  ✅ Respecting individual rights

DEMOCRACY IS NOT NECESSARILY BETTER AT:
  ⚠️ Economic growth (dictatorships sometimes grow faster)
  ⚠️ Reducing economic inequality
  ⚠️ Quick decision-making
```

## Common Exam Traps ⚠️

1. Writing one-sided answers (democracy is perfect) — WRONG
2. Not acknowledging limitations while defending democracy
3. Forgetting the dignity/equality angle
