# P-Ch02 — Federalism ⭐

> **Political Science** | **Marks**: 4-5 | [[🌍 Social Science]]
> ⭐ **HIGH YIELD** — Lists, decentralization, Panchayati Raj

## Key Concepts

- **Federal**: power divided between central and state governments
- **Unitary**: power concentrated at center
- India: "holding together" federation (not "coming together" like USA)

## Three Lists (7th Schedule)

| List | Examples | Who makes laws? |
|------|----------|----------------|
| Union | Defence, banking, foreign affairs, currency, communications | Central govt only |
| State | Police, trade, agriculture, irrigation, land | State govt only |
| Concurrent | Education, forests, marriage, adoption | Both (Central prevails) |
| Residuary | Cyber laws, e-commerce | Central govt |

## Decentralization (73rd/74th Amendment, 1992)

```
THREE-TIER PANCHAYATI RAJ:
  Village: Gram Panchayat (elected by Gram Sabha)
  Block: Panchayat Samiti
  District: Zila Parishad

URBAN:
  Municipality (town)
  Municipal Corporation (city)

KEY FEATURES:
  - Regular elections by State Election Commission
  - 1/3 seats reserved for women
  - Reservation for SC/ST
  - State Finance Commission for funds
```

## Common Exam Traps ⚠️

1. Confusing Union and State list subjects
2. Not knowing that education is in CONCURRENT list
3. Forgetting 73rd/74th Amendment year (1992)
