# P-Ch03 — Gender, Religion and Caste

> **Political Science** | **Marks**: 3-4 | [[🌍 Social Science]]

## Gender in Politics

- Women's representation in Parliament: ~15% (low)
- Panchayati Raj: 1/3 seats reserved for women (73rd Amendment)
- Gender issues: domestic violence, dowry, unequal wages, stereotypes

## Religion in Politics

- **Secularism**: no official religion, freedom to practice any religion
- **Communalism**: using religion for political gain → dangerous
- Constitution: Articles 25-28 guarantee religious freedom

## Caste in Politics

```
CASTE IN POLITICS (negative):
  - Vote banks, caste-based parties
  - Caste tensions during elections
  
POLITICS IN CASTE (positive):
  - Reservation for disadvantaged groups
  - Constitutional provisions for equality
  - OBC Commission, SC/ST Commissions
```

## Common Exam Traps ⚠️

1. Not differentiating "caste in politics" vs "politics in caste"
2. Forgetting secularism provisions in Constitution
3. One-sided answers (must show both positive and negative)
