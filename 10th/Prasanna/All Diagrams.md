# All Diagrams (Science)

> **Quick Reference Guide** | Practice drawing these weekly | [[🔬 Science]]

## Biology (Highest Weightage for Diagrams)

1. **Human Digestive System**: Label Liver, Gall Bladder, Pancreas, Stomach, Small/Large Intestine.
2. **Human Respiratory System**: Label Trachea, Bronchi, Alveoli, Diaphragm.
3. **Human Heart**: Cross-section. Label all 4 chambers, Vena Cava, Aorta, Pulmonary Artery/Vein. Draw arrows for blood flow.
4. **Human Excretory System & Nephron**: Label Bowman's Capsule, Glomerulus, Tubular part.
5. **Structure of a Neuron**: Label Dendrite, Cell Body, Axon, Nerve Ending.
6. **Human Brain**: Sagittal section. Label Cerebrum, Cerebellum, Medulla, Hypothalamus.
7. **Reflex Arc**: Pathway from receptor to effector.
8. **Flower Structure**: L.S. of a flower. Label Stigma, Style, Ovary, Anther, Filament.
9. **Male & Female Reproductive Systems**.
10. **Cross Section of a Leaf**: Label Stomata, Guard cells, Upper/Lower Epidermis.

## Physics

1. **Ray Diagrams for Concave Mirror** (6 cases)
2. **Ray Diagrams for Convex Lens** (6 cases)
3. **Refraction through a Glass Slab**: Label incident ray, refracted ray, emergent ray, lateral displacement.
4. **Refraction through a Prism**: Dispersion of white light into VIBGYOR.
5. **Human Eye**: Label Cornea, Iris, Pupil, Lens, Retina, Optic Nerve.
6. **Defects of Vision**: Myopia (and correction with concave lens), Hypermetropia (and correction with convex lens).
7. **Electric Circuit Diagram**: Include battery, switch, ammeter (series), voltmeter (parallel), resistor.
8. **Magnetic Field Lines**: Around a bar magnet, straight conductor, circular loop, and solenoid.
9. **Electric Motor**: Label Armature, Strong Magnets, Split Rings, Brushes.
10. **Electric Generator (AC)**: Label Armature, Magnets, Slip Rings, Brushes.

## Chemistry

1. **Electrolysis of Water**: Label Anode, Cathode, H₂ gas, O₂ gas.
2. **Action of Steam on a Metal**.
3. **Testing pH of solutions**.
4. **Formation of Micelles** (Soap action).

*Tip: Always use a sharp pencil for diagrams and a ruler for label lines. All label lines should ideally point to the right side of the diagram.*
