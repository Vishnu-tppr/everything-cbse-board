## 📐 Mathematics (Guaranteed 5M Patterns)

1.  **Matrices & Determinants:** Solving a system of linear equations using the Inverse Matrix method. (95% Probability).
2.  **Calculus (AOI):** Finding the area of a region bounded by a circle/parabola and a line. (90% Probability).
3.  **3D Geometry:** Finding the **Shortest Distance** between two skew lines or the foot of the perpendicular.
4.  **Probability:** Bayes' Theorem application (e.g., "A person speaks truth 3 out of 4 times...").
5.  **Relations & Functions:** Proving a function is **Bijective** (One-to-One and Onto) or an **Equivalence Relation**.
