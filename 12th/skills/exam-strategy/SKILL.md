## The "Senior Sibling" Time Management

### For 70-Mark Papers (Physics, Chemistry, Bio, CS)
*Total Time: 180 Minutes*

| Section | Questions | Time | Focus |
|---------|-----------|------|-------|
| **Section A (MCQ)** | 18 | 30-35 min | 1.5-2 min per Q. No rambling. |
| **Section B (SA-I)** | 7 | 25-30 min | 2-mark precise definitions/numericals. |
| **Section C (SA-II)** | 5 | 35-40 min | 3-mark conceptual/mechanisms. |
| **Section D (Case)** | 2 | 20-25 min | Data extraction & application. |
| **Section E (LA)** | 3 | 45-50 min | 5-mark derivations/structured answers. |
| **Buffer/Review** | - | 10-15 min | Units, Arrows, and Boxed Results. |

## 📐 Presentation Rules for 12th Grade
1. **Derivations:** Diagram (Left) $\to$ Theory (Right) $\to$ Step-wise derivation $\to$ Final Boxed Formula.
2. **Organic Chemistry:** Show the **Intermediate** state if the mechanism is asked. Use curly arrows for electron flow.
3. **Math:** Mention the property used (e.g., "Using Property P4 of Integrals").
4. **Biology:** Use a **Dark HB Pencil** for diagrams. Labeling should be on one side (Right) whenever possible.

## 🧠 Mental Edge & Discipline
- **The "No-Zero" Day:** Even on bad days, solve 5 Math problems or 2 Derivations.
- **Active Recall:** After every chapter, close the book and write down the "Formula Web" from memory.
- **Exam Simulation:** Sit for 3 hours *without* phone or breaks once a week. Stamina is as important as knowledge.
