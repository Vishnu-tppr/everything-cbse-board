## ✍️ Presentation Rules (Senior Sibling)
- **The Box:** Enclose your diagram in a neat pencil-drawn box.
- **Single-Side Labeling:** Draw all labels on the **Right** side with straight horizontal lines.
- **Arrows:** For Physics, arrows on light rays and current paths are **Non-negotiable**.
- **Scale:** A diagram should be roughly **1/3 of a page**. Never draw "micro-diagrams".

**Command Integration:** Use `/diagram-drill` to get a list of diagrams to practice for a specific chapter.
