# Project Templates — Securing the 30/30 Practicals

In 12th Grade, your Investigative Project is a major component of the Practical exam. A high-quality, research-oriented project ensures a smooth Viva and full marks from the External Examiner.

---

## 🧪 Chemistry Investigative Project (30M Practical)

### Suggested High-Score Topics
- **Food Analysis:** Presence of Oxalate ions in Guava fruit at different stages of ripening.
- **Water Quality:** Analysis of various samples of drinking water for hardness, iron, and chloride.
- **Biochemistry:** Comparative study of the amount of Caffeine in different samples of Tea.
- **Surface Chemistry:** Study of the effect of Metal Coupling on the rusting of Iron.

### Standard Format
1. **Title Page:** Title, Name, Roll No (CBSE), School.
2. **Certificate:** Mandatory wording from CBSE teacher.
3. **Acknowledgment.**
4. **Index.**
5. **Theory:** Concepts behind the experiment (e.g., Titration logic).
6. **Apparatus & Chemicals.**
7. **Procedure:** Step-by-step description.
8. **Observations:** Table format with raw data.
9. **Result & Conclusion.**
10. **Bibliography.**

## 🧬 Biology Investigative Project (30M Practical)

### Suggested High-Score Topics
- **Human Health:** Study of Drug/Alcohol addiction patterns and physiological effects.
- **Genetics:** Study of Mendelian inheritance using seeds/flowers.
- **Ecology:** Analysis of air/water pollution levels in your locality using Bio-indicators.
- **Microbiology:** Study of the rate of fermentation of various fruit juices.

---

## 🔭 Physics Investigative Project (30M Practical)

### Suggested High-Score Topics
- **Semiconductors:** Constructing a Full-wave Rectifier using Diodes.
- **Electromagnetism:** Building a working model of a Step-up/Step-down Transformer.
- **Optics:** Determination of Refractive Index of different liquids using a Hollow Prism.
- **Logic Gates:** Constructing basic Logic Gates (AND, OR, NOT) using transistors.

---

## 💻 Computer Science Project (30M Practical)

### Framework
- **Requirement:** A Python-SQL integrated application.
- **Topics:** Hospital Management System, Library Management, Railway Reservation.
- **Mandatory:** Use of Binary Files (Pickle) or CSV files, and at least 3-4 SQL tables with Joins.

## ✍️ The "Senior Sibling" Viva Hack
- **Know Your Sources:** Don't just copy. The External Examiner will ask "Where did you get this data?".
- **Handwritten vs Printed:** Most schools allow printed for 12th, but check with your teacher. A well-organized spiral binding is standard.
- **The "Safety" Question:** In Chemistry/Biology, be ready to explain the safety precautions you took.
