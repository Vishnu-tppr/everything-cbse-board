# NEET Strategy — The 720 Roadmap

To crack NEET with a top rank while securing 495+ in CBSE Boards, you must master the art of **Dual-Mode Preparation**. You aren't studying two different things; you are studying one NCERT in two different ways.

## 1. The NCERT Extraction Method

NEET is a game of "NCERT Line Retrieval". 90% of Biology and 85% of Chemistry comes directly from the textbook.

### 🧬 Biology (The 360/360 Goal)
- **Active Reading:** Don't just read; annotate. Identify the "Exceptions" (e.g., "All birds have X, EXCEPT Y"). NEET loves exceptions.
- **Diagram Labeling:** Every label in NCERT is a potential 4-mark question. Practice labeling 'blind' using the `Tutor` agent.
- **Summary & Blue Boxes:** These often contain the high-logic facts that appear in Assertion-Reason questions.

### 🧪 Chemistry (The 180/180 Goal)
- **Inorganic:** No side-books needed. Just memorize the NCERT trends, colors of ions, and catalyst names.
- **Organic:** If you can do the 12th-grade mechanisms for Boards, you have the logic for NEET. Focus on the "Name Reactions" and "Distinction Tests".
- **Physical:** Focus on "Formula Speed". You have 45 seconds per question.

## 3. The 45-Second Rule (Speed Drills)

NEET is a speed test. You have 200 minutes for 200 questions (attempt 180).
- **Biology:** Target 30-35 minutes for 90 questions.
- **Chemistry:** Target 45-50 minutes for 45 questions.
- **Physics:** Target 60-70 minutes for 45 questions.
- **Buffer:** Always keep 15-20 minutes for OMR bubbling and final check.

---

## 4. Balancing Boards & NEET (The Calendar)

### Phase 1 (April - October): Concept Depth
- Focus on deep NCERT reading. 
- Use `/explain` for every complex derivation. 
- Solve 50 MCQs per chapter after the Board-level study.

### Phase 2 (November - January): Practical & Mock Prep
- Finish your 30-mark practical files using `Practical-Check`.
- Start doing "Chapter-Wise Mocks" on weekends.
- Use `/neet-mcq` for assertion-reasoning practice.

### Phase 3 (February - March): Board Sprint
- Shift 80% focus to Board presentation.
- Solve 10 years of PYQs.
- Use `/mark-my-answer` for every long-answer practice.

### Phase 4 (April - May): NEET Final Push
- 100% MCQ mode.
- Full-length mocks every 2 days.
- Revision of "Volatile Facts" (Inorganic and Bio examples).

## 6. The "Silly Mistake" Audit

Use the **Weak-Spotter** agent to track:
- **Calculation Errors:** Especially in Physical Chemistry.
- **"Not" Trap:** Missing the word "Incorrect" or "Not" in the question.
- **Unit Trap:** Using $cm$ instead of $m$ in Physics.

---

## 7. Psychological Topper Edge
- **The -1 Fear:** Don't guess. In NEET, a wrong guess is a 5-mark loss (+4 you missed, -1 penalty).
- **Skip Logic:** If a Physics numerical looks like it takes 3 minutes, SKIP it and move to Bio. Come back later.
- **Consistency:** 6 hours of daily focus is better than 14 hours of erratic study.
