# IA & Practical Optimizer — Grade 12 (Batch 2026-27)

## The High-Stakes Component
- **PCMB/PCMC Sciences:** 30 Marks (Practical) + 70 Marks (Theory) = 100
- **Mathematics/English:** 20 Marks (Internal) + 80 Marks (Theory) = 100

Targeting **30/30** and **20/20** is non-negotiable for a 495+ score.

---

## 🔬 Science Practicals (30 Marks) — Phys, Chem, Bio, CS

The external examiner determines your score. First impressions and record quality are everything.

| Component | Marks | Focus Area |
|-----------|-------|------------|
| **Two Experiments** | 7 + 7 = 14 | Procedure, Calculations, Result with Units. |
| **Practical Record** | 5 | Neatness, Index completion, Teacher signatures. |
| **Investigatory Project**| 3 | Originality, Bibliography, Viva on the topic. |
| **Viva-Voce** | 5 | Conceptual depth and confidence. |
| **Spotting (Bio only)** | 7 | Speed and specific technical identification. |

### Topper Tactics for 30/30:
1. **The Viva Edge:** External examiners often ask "What is your favorite chapter?". Be ready with one chapter mastered deep.
2. **Error Analysis:** If your reading is slightly off, don't fake it. Show "Precautions" to justify the deviation.
3. **Log Tables:** Master the use of log tables for Physics calculations.
4. **Spotting (Bio):** Practice identifying the slides/specimens in under 1 minute.

## 📐 Mathematics & English IA (20 Marks)

Internal marks are managed by your school teachers.

### Mathematics (20M)
- **Periodic Tests (best 2 of 3):** 10 Marks.
- **Math Lab Activities:** 10 Marks (Record book + Activities + Viva).

### English Core (20M)
- **ASL (Assessment of Speaking & Listening):** 10 Marks. (Focus on fluency and interactive competence).
- **Project Work:** 10 Marks. (A research-based file on a literary theme).

## 📅 Action Timeline (Batch 2026-27)

### Phase 1 (April - July): The Foundation
- □ Start Lab Records from Week 1. Never let a backlog exceed 2 experiments.
- □ Choose your **Investigatory Project** topic early.
- □ Mathematics: Ensure all notebook assignments are signed.

### Phase 2 (August - October): The Mid-Term Push
- □ Complete the first draft of your Project Report.
- □ Finish at least 60% of the mandatory experiments.
- □ English ASL: Start practicing your 2-minute speech.

### Phase 3 (November - January): The Final Polish
- □ Get the Final Project File spiral-bound or in a neat folder.
- □ Conduct a "Mock Practical" using the `/practical-check` command.
- □ Ensure the **Index** of your Record book is 100% updated and signed.

## 📜 The "Senior Sibling" Viva Drill
- **Eye Contact:** Look the external examiner in the eye.
- **Precision:** Don't ramble. Give the definition, then the formula.
- **Honesty:** If you don't know an answer, say "I am unable to recall at the moment, sir/ma'am," rather than guessing wildly.
- **Dress Code:** Full, crisp uniform, trimmed hair/nails, and a clean lab coat. This counts for "Professionalism".
