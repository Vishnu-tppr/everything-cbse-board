# Tamil Core — Grade 12

Tamil is often the "Scoring" subject that boosts your total to 495+. Don't ignore it.

---

## 📚 Paper Structure
1. **Section A (Reading):** Unseen passages and poems (Reading Comprehension).
2. **Section B (Writing):** Essay writing, Letter writing, and Creative composition.
3. **Section C (Grammar):** Punnarchi, Bagupadham, and Sentence transformation.
4. **Section D (Literature):** Detailed and Non-detailed texts (Seyyul & Urainadai).

---

## ✍️ Writing Strategy
- **Essay (Katturai):** Use relevant sub-headings. Start with a relevant **Thirukkural** or a quote from a Tamil scholar.
- **Letter (Kadidham):** Follow the formal format (Anuppunar, Perunar, Porul).
- **Tamil Typing/Handwriting:** Ensure clarity in "Othira" and "Pulli" placements.

---

## 📖 Literature Focus
- **Seyyul (Poetry):** Memorize the "Manappadam" songs. You must know the meaning and the context (Sondharppam).
- **Urainadai (Prose):** Focus on the main characters and the moral of the story.

---

## 🧪 Grammar (Ilakkanam)
- **Punarchi Vidhi:** Master the rules for joining words.
- **Pagupadham:** Learn to split words into Pagudhi, Vigudhi, Idainilai, etc.
- **Sentence Types:** Tan-vinai, Pirar-vinai, Sei-vinai, Seyappaattu-vinai.

---

## ⏱️ Time Management
- Section A: 30 Mins.
- Section B: 45 Mins.
- Section C: 30 Mins.
- Section D: 75 Mins.

**Senior Sibling Tip:** Use "Sandhu" (Conjunctions) correctly to make your Tamil prose flow elegantly. This impresses the examiner.
