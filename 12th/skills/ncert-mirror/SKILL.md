# NCERT Language Mirror (Grade 12)

CBSE examiners use the NCERT textbook as the **Marking Bible**. If your answer uses coaching-centre jargon or vague synonyms instead of NCERT technical terms, you will lose marks even if your logic is correct.

---

## 📖 NCERT Style Rules

### Rule 1: Technical Accuracy over Simple English
```text
❌ "The heart pumps blood faster" 
✅ "Increased cardiac output and stroke volume" (Biology)

❌ "Light bends when it enters water"
✅ "Refraction of light occurs due to change in optical density" (Physics)

❌ "Carbon forms 4 bonds"
✅ "Tetravalency and Catenation properties of Carbon" (Chemistry)
```

### Rule 2: Mechanism and Process Names
- Use the **Exact Name** provided in NCERT. 
- *Example:* Use "Semiconservative replication of DNA" instead of "Half-copying of DNA".
- *Example:* Use "Nucleophilic Addition-Elimination" instead of "Adding and removing a group".

### Rule 3: Eliminating "Coaching Jargon"
The following terms are common in coaching but should be used carefully in Board exams:
- **Physics:** Use "Potential Difference" and "Electromotive Force (emf)" as defined in NCERT.
- **Chemistry:** Use "Resonance Stabilization" and "Inductive Effect" exactly as phrased in the text.
- **Math:** State properties like "The King Property" as "Property IV of Definite Integrals" (or state the integral equality).

## 🔭 Subject-Specific Mirror Targets

### 🧬 Biology
- Use *Microsporogenesis*, *Megasporogenesis*, *Parthenocarpy*.
- Avoid vague terms like "pollen creation" or "seedless fruit".

### 🧪 Chemistry
- Use *Enantiomers*, *Racemisation*, *Electrophilic Substitution*.
- NCERT definitions for *Azeotropes* and *Colligative Properties* must be memorized.

### 🔭 Physics
- Use *Gaussian Surface*, *Phasor Diagram*, *Total Internal Reflection*.
- Explain the *Caspain* or *Wavefront* concepts exactly as the NCERT does.

### 📖 English Core
- Use thematic keywords from the NCERT's "Thinking about Language" sections.
- *Example:* "Hamels' linguistic chauvinism" or "The existential irony in The Rattrap".
