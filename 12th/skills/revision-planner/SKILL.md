# Revision Planner — The 495+ Schedule

Revision in 12th grade is not just "re-reading". It is about **Active Recall** and **Pattern Mastery**.

---

## 📅 The Three-Pass Revision System

### Pass 1: "The Base Build" (Nov - Dec)
- **Goal:** 100% NCERT coverage + RD Sharma/SL Arora selective numericals.
- **Action:** Solve every intext question and back exercise. 
- **Output:** Your own "Formula Master Sheet" and "Mechanism Map".

### Pass 2: "The Mock Marathon" (Jan - Feb)
- **Goal:** Exam stamina and speed.
- **Action:** Solve 10 full-length sample papers per subject. 
- **Output:** A updated **Mistake-DNA** tracker for every mock.

### Pass 3: "The Volatile Sweep" (Final 48 Hours)
- **Goal:** Lock in facts that evaporate quickly.
- **Action:** Review Inorganic trends, Named Reactions, Bio examples, and CS syntax.
- **Output:** Calm mind and muscle memory for derivations.

## 🚀 Revision Command: `/revision-plan`
Invoke this command with your exam date and current weak chapters to get a day-by-day countdown:

```bash
/revision-plan 2027-03-01 "Integration, Ray Optics, Organic Mechanisms"
```
The AI will calculate the remaining days and generate a **Spaced Repetition** schedule for you.
