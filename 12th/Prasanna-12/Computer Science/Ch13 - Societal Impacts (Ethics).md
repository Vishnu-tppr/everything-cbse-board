# 🛡️ Ch13 - Societal Impacts (Ethics)

> **Computer Science** | **Marks**: ~10 | [[💻 Computer Science]]
> This chapter covers digital footprints, cyber-safety, intellectual property rights, and the environmental impact of technology.

---

## 🎯 Conceptual Core (NCERT Mirror)

### 1. Digital Footprint
- **Passive**: Data collected without user's active participation (IP address, location).
- **Active**: Data shared intentionally by user (Posts, emails).

### 2. Intellectual Property Rights (IPR)
- **Copyright**: Protection for artistic/literary work.
- **Patent**: Protection for inventions.
- **Trademark**: Protection for logos/brand names.
- **Plagiarism**: Copying someone else's work and presenting it as your own.

---

## 📐 The 12th Grade Technicals

### 🧪 Cyber Safety
- **Phishing**: Fraudulent emails to steal sensitive info.
- **Cyber Bullying**: Harassment using digital technology.
- **Identity Theft**: Impersonating someone using their personal data.
- **Firewalls & Antivirus**: Basic protection tools.

### ⚡ E-Waste and Environment
- **E-waste**: Discarded electronic devices.
- **Disposal**: Recycle, Refurbish, and Reuse. Proper disposal is critical as e-waste contains toxic substances like Lead and Mercury.

---

## ⚠️ The "Mistake DNA" Alert (Common Traps)
- **Free vs Open Source**: 
  - **Free Software**: Can be free of cost but source code might not be available.
  - **Open Source (OSS)**: Source code is available for modification.
- **Proprietary Software**: Owned by a company; source code is secret (e.g., MS Office).
- **Digital Divide**: Gap between people who have access to technology and those who don't.
