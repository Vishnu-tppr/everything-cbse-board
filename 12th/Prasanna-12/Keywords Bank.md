# 🧠 Master Keywords Bank (CBSE 12th)

> Examiners look for specific **NCERT Keywords**. Using these instead of common synonyms can be the difference between a 4/5 and a 5/5.

---

## 🔭 Physics (Conceptual Precision)
- **Equipotential Surface**: No work is done in moving a charge along this surface.
- **Drift Velocity**: Average velocity of electrons under an electric field.
- **Mutual Induction**: Production of induced EMF in a coil due to current change in a neighboring coil.
- **Total Internal Reflection**: Happens when angle of incidence > critical angle.
- **Binding Energy per Nucleon**: Measure of nuclear stability.

---

## 🧪 Chemistry (Mechanism & Logic)
- **Zwitterion**: Dipolar ion form of amino acids.
- **Lanthanoid Contraction**: Steady decrease in atomic/ionic radii of lanthanoid elements.
- **Enantiomers**: Non-superimposable mirror images.
- **Pseudo First Order**: Reaction that appears to be of a higher order but follows first-order kinetics.
- **Chelate Effect**: Enhanced stability of complexes containing chelate ligands.

---

## 📐 Mathematics (Theorem & Logic)
- **Equivalence Relation**: Reflexive, Symmetric, and Transitive.
- **Point of Inflexion**: Where the second derivative $f''(x) = 0$ and changes sign.
- **Skew Lines**: Neither parallel nor intersecting in 3D space.
- **Objective Function**: The function to be maximized or minimized in LPP.
- **Conditional Probability**: $P(A|B)$ — Probability of A given B has occurred.

---

## 🧬 Biology (NCERT Terminology)
- **Parthenocarpy**: Fruit development without fertilization.
- **Semiconservative Replication**: Meselson and Stahl's model of DNA.
- **RNA Interference (RNAi)**: Cellular defense mechanism in eukaryotes.
- **Primary Productivity**: Rate of biomass production by producers.
- **Founder Effect**: Genetic drift in a small colonized population.

---

## 💻 Computer Science (Technical Terms)
- **Pickle Module**: Used for serializing/de-serializing Python objects (Binary files).
- **LEGB Rule**: Local, Enclosed, Global, Built-in scope hierarchy.
- **Referential Integrity**: Relationship between foreign key and primary key in SQL.
- **Aggregate Functions**: SUM, AVG, COUNT, MIN, MAX (applied on multiple rows).
- **Bandwidth**: Data transfer capacity of a network channel.

---

## 📖 English Core (Literature Nuance)
- **Linguistic Chauvinism**: Pride in one's own language (The Last Lesson).
- **Vicious Circle**: The cycle of exploitation by middlemen (Lost Spring).
- **Irony**: The discrepancy between expectation and reality (The Tiger King).
- **Anachronism**: Something out of its proper time (The Third Level).
- **Stoicism**: Indifference to pain or pleasure (The Enemy).
