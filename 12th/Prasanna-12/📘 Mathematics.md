## 🚀 The Calculus Core (35 Marks)

Calculus is the foundation of 12th Math. Focus on:
- **Integration**: Partial Fractions, By Parts, Properties of Definite Integrals ($P_4$ is high-yield).
- **Differential Equations**: Variable separable, Homogeneous, Linear equations.
- **Applications**: Area under curves (Simple), Maxima/Minima (Word problems - 5M guaranteed).

---

## 📐 Geometry & Vectors (14 Marks)
- **Vectors**: Dot and Cross products, Projection of a vector.
- **3D Geometry**: Shortest distance between skew lines (5M formula-based), Equations of lines.
