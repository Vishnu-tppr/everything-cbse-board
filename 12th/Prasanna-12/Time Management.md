# ⏱️ 12th Grade Time Management Protocol

> In 12th grade, the papers are long. If you don't manage your time, you will leave 5-10 marks unattempted. Use the **180-Minute Pacing Rule**.

---

## 🕒 70-Mark Pacing (Physics/Chemistry/Biology)
*Total Time: 180 Mins + 15 Mins Reading*

| Section | Questions | Weightage | Target Time | Logic |
|---------|-----------|-----------|-------------|-------|
| **Reading**| - | - | 15 Mins | Analyze choices & solve MCQs mentally |
| **Section A**| 16 | 16M | 25 Mins | Rapid fire MCQs & Assertion-Reason |
| **Section B**| 5 | 10M | 20 Mins | Short Answer-I (2M each) |
| **Section C**| 7 | 21M | 45 Mins | Short Answer-II (3M each) |
| **Section D**| 2 | 8M | 25 Mins | Case-Based (4M each) - Read carefully |
| **Section E**| 3 | 15M | 45 Mins | Long Answer (5M each) - Derivations |
| **Review** | - | - | 20 Mins | Units, Diagrams, & Error check |

---

## 🕒 80-Mark Pacing (Mathematics/English)
*Total Time: 180 Mins + 15 Mins Reading*

| Section | weightage | Target Time | Strategy |
|---------|-----------|-------------|----------|
| **Reading** | - | 15 Mins | Plan the 5-mark LA choice |
| **Section A** | 20M | 35 Mins | Direct formula application |
| **Section B/C**| 30M | 60 Mins | Mid-level logic & proofs |
| **Section D** | 20M | 50 Mins | 5-Mark Heavyweights (Calculus/3D) |
| **Section E** | 10M | 20 Mins | Case-Based / LPP |
| **Review** | - | 15 Mins | Calculation check ($+C$ in integrals) |

---

## 🧐 The 15-Minute Reading Time Strategy
1. **Min 1-5**: Scan the paper for "Shockers" (unseen questions). Mark the optional questions you will attempt.
2. **Min 6-10**: Solve the Assertion-Reason questions mentally. They take time during writing; pre-solving them saves 5 mins later.
3. **Min 11-15**: Read the **Case Study** passage. It's usually a long read. Knowing the context beforehand allows you to write the answer instantly when you reach that section.

---

## ✅ The "Topper Revision" Checklist (Last 20 Mins)
- [ ] **Units**: Did I write $F, \Omega, m/s$ everywhere?
- [ ] **Boxes**: Are all final numerical answers boxed?
- [ ] **Diagrams**: Are the ray/circuit/biology diagrams labeled in pen?
- [ ] **Questions**: Did I write the correct question number for the optional choice I picked?
