## 🚀 High-Yield Units

### 1. 🔭 Optics (18 Marks)
- **Ray Optics**: Lens Maker's Formula, Prism, Optical Instruments (Microscope/Telescope).
- **Wave Optics**: YDSE, Huygens' Principle (Proof of Reflection/Refraction).

### 2. ⚡ Electrostatics & Current (16 Marks)
- **Gauss Law Applications**: Infinite wire, thin sheet, shell.
- **Current**: Drift velocity derivation, Kirchhoff's Laws (KVL/KCL), Wheatstone Bridge.

### 3. 🧲 Magnetism & AC (17 Marks)
- **Biot-Savart Law**: Field on axis of circular loop.
- **AC**: LCR Series circuit, Resonance, Transformer logic.
