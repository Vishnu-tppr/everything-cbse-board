# 👶 Ch03 - Human Reproduction

> **Biology** | **Marks**: ~6-8 | [[🧬 Biology]]
> This chapter analyzes the human reproductive system, gamete production, menstrual cycle, and the events leading to birth.

---

## 🎯 Conceptual Core (NCERT Mirror)

### 1. Reproductive Systems
- **Male**: Testes (Extra-abdominal in scrotum), Seminiferous tubules (Sperm production), Leydig cells (Secretes androgens).
- **Female**: Ovaries, Fallopian tubes (site of fertilization), Uterus (site of implantation), Mammary glands.

### 2. Gametogenesis
- **Spermatogenesis**: Continuous process; one spermatogonium $\to$ 4 sperms.
- **Oogenesis**: Discontinuous process; starts in fetal stage, arrested at prophase I till puberty. One oogonium $\to$ 1 ovum + 3 polar bodies.

---

## 📐 The 12th Grade Technicals

### 🧪 Menstrual Cycle (Critical)
1. **Menstrual Phase** (1-5 days): Breakdown of endometrium.
2. **Follicular Phase**: FSH stimulates follicle growth; Estrogen secretes.
3. **Ovulation** (Day 14): **LH Surge** triggers release of ovum.
4. **Luteal Phase**: Corpus luteum secretes **Progesterone** (maintains endometrium).

### ⚡ Fertilization & Implantation
- **Fertilization**: Occurs at Ampullary-isthmic junction.
- **Blastocyst**: Stage that implants into the uterine wall.
- **Placenta**: Endocrine tissue secreting hCG, hPL, Estrogen, Progesterone.

---

## ⚠️ The "Mistake DNA" Alert (Common Traps)
- **LH Surge**: The precise cause of ovulation. Without LH surge, ovulation won't happen.
- **Sperm Entry**: Triggers the completion of Meiosis II in the secondary oocyte.
- **hCG**: The hormone detected in pregnancy tests; produced ONLY by the placenta.
- **Colostrum**: Milk produced during initial days, rich in IgA antibodies (provides passive immunity).
