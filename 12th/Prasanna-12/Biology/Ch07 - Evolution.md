# 🐒 Ch07 - Evolution

> **Biology** | **Marks**: ~4 | [[🧬 Biology]]
> This chapter explores the origin of life, theories of evolution, and the evidence supporting biological change over time.

---

## 🎯 Conceptual Core (NCERT Mirror)

### 1. Origin of Life
- **Big Bang Theory**: Origin of universe.
- **Miller’s Experiment**: Simulated primitive earth conditions ($CH_4, NH_3, H_2, H_2O$ at high temp + electric discharge). Produced **Amino acids**.
- **Chemical Evolution**: Oparin and Haldane proposed life came from pre-existing non-living organic molecules.

### 2. Evidences for Evolution
- **Paleontological**: Fossils in different sedimentary layers.
- **Comparative Anatomy**:
  - **Homologous Structures**: Same origin, different function (e.g., forelimbs of whale, bat, human). Proves **Divergent Evolution**.
  - **Analogous Structures**: Different origin, same function (e.g., wings of butterfly and bird). Proves **Convergent Evolution**.
- **Biochemical**: Similarities in proteins and genes.

---

## 📐 The 12th Grade Technicals

### 🧪 Theories of Evolution
- **Lamarckism**: Use and disuse of organs (Giraffe neck). [Disproved].
- **Darwinism**: **Natural Selection**. Branching descent and natural selection are the two key concepts.
- **Mutation Theory (Hugo de Vries)**: Evolution occurs by sudden large mutations (**Saltation**), not gradual variations.

### ⚡ Hardy-Weinberg Principle
- Genetic equilibrium: $p^2 + 2pq + q^2 = 1$.
- Factors affecting equilibrium: Gene migration, Genetic drift, Mutation, Recombination, Natural Selection.

---

## ⚠️ The "Mistake DNA" Alert (Common Traps)
- **Homologous vs Analogous**: Always link Homologous $\to$ Divergent and Analogous $\to$ Convergent.
- **Miller's Gases**: Remember the ratio and the fact that **Oxygen was absent** (Reducing atmosphere).
- **Adaptive Radiation**: Evolution of different species in a given geographical area starting from a point (e.g., Darwin’s Finches, Australian Marsupials).
