# 💊 Ch08 - Human Health and Disease

> **Biology** | **Marks**: ~7-9 | [[🧬 Biology]]
> This chapter covers common human diseases, the immune system, and the challenges of AIDS, Cancer, and drug abuse.

---

## 🎯 Conceptual Core (NCERT Mirror)

### 1. Common Human Diseases
- **Bacterial**: Typhoid (*Salmonella typhi* - Widal test), Pneumonia (*Streptococcus pneumoniae*).
- **Viral**: Common Cold (Rhino viruses).
- **Protozoan**: **Malaria** (*Plasmodium* - Malignant is *P. falciparum*), Amoebiasis.
- **Fungal**: Ringworm (*Microsporum*).
- **Helminthic**: Ascariasis, Elephantiasis (*Wuchereria bancrofti*).

### 2. Immunity
- **Innate**: Non-specific; 4 barriers (Physical, Physiological, Cellular, Cytokine).
- **Acquired**: Specific; Memory-based.
  - **B-cells**: Produce antibodies (Humoral immune response).
  - **T-cells**: Cell-mediated immunity (CMI) - responsible for **graft rejection**.
- **Active vs Passive**: Active (self-produced antibodies), Passive (ready-made, e.g., Colostrum).

---

## 📐 The 12th Grade Technicals

### 🧪 AIDS & Cancer
- **AIDS**: Caused by HIV (Retrovirus). Attacks **Helper T-cells**. Diagnosis: **ELISA**.
- **Cancer**: Breakdown of regulatory mechanisms. 
  - **Contact Inhibition**: Lost in cancer cells.
  - **Metastasis**: Property where tumor cells spread to distant sites (most feared property).
  - **Diagnosis**: Biopsy, MRI, CT.

### ⚡ Drugs and Alcohol Abuse
- **Opioids**: Morphine (Heroin/Smack). Depressant.
- **Cannabinoids**: Effect on cardiovascular system.
- **Coca Alkaloids**: Cocaine (Coke/Crack). Interference with Dopamine.

---

## ⚠️ The "Mistake DNA" Alert (Common Traps)
- **Malaria Life Cycle**: Sexual stage occurs in the **Mosquito** (primary host); Asexual stage in **Human**.
- **Antibody Structure**: $H_2L_2$ (two heavy, two light chains).
- **Innate vs Acquired**: Innate is present from birth; Acquired is developed during lifetime.
- **Allergies**: Produced by **IgE** antibodies and histamine/serotonin from mast cells.
