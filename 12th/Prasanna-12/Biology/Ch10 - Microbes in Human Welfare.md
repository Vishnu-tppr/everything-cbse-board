# 🧫 Ch10 - Microbes in Human Welfare

> **Biology** | **Marks**: ~4-5 | [[🧬 Biology]]
> This chapter highlights the beneficial roles of microorganisms in household products, industrial processes, sewage treatment, and energy production.

---

## 🎯 Conceptual Core (NCERT Mirror)

### 1. Household & Industrial Products
- **Household**: 
  - *Lactobacillus* (Curd - increases Vit B12).
  - *Saccharomyces cerevisiae* (Baker’s yeast - bread).
- **Antibiotics**: Alexander Fleming discovered **Penicillin** from *Penicillium notatum*.
- **Chemicals/Enzymes**:
  - Citric Acid (*Aspergillus niger*).
  - Acetic Acid (*Acetobacter aceti*).
  - Statins (*Monascus purpureus* - lowers blood cholesterol).
  - Cyclosporin A (*Trichoderma polysporum* - immunosuppressant for organ transplant).

### 2. Sewage Treatment (Critical)
- **Primary Treatment**: Physical removal of particles (filtration/sedimentation).
- **Secondary Treatment (Biological)**: 
  - Use of **Flocs** (bacteria + fungal filaments).
  - Reduces **BOD** (Biochemical Oxygen Demand).
  - Anaerobic sludge digesters produce **Biogas**.

---

## 📐 The 12th Grade Technicals

### 🧪 Biogas Production
- Produced by **Methanogens** (*Methanobacterium*) in anaerobic conditions.
- Components: $CH_4, CO_2, H_2$.
- Rural India setup: IARI and KVIC developed the technology.

### ⚡ Bio-control Agents & Bio-fertilizers
- **Bio-control**: Use of *Bacillus thuringiensis* (Bt) for caterpillars, *Trichoderma* for plant pathogens.
- **Bio-fertilizers**: Nitrogen fixing bacteria (*Rhizobium, Azospirillum*), Mycorrhiza (*Glomus* - phosphorus absorption), Cyanobacteria (*Anabaena, Nostoc*).

---

## ⚠️ The "Mistake DNA" Alert (Common Traps)
- **BOD Logic**: High BOD = More polluting water (higher organic matter).
- **Statins vs Cyclosporin**: 
  - Statins $\to$ Cholesterol.
  - Cyclosporin $\to$ Organ Transplant.
- **Yeast Type**: *S. cerevisiae* is used for both brewing (Brewer's yeast) and baking (Baker's yeast).
