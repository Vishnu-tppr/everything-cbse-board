# 🏥 Ch04 - Reproductive Health

> **Biology** | **Marks**: ~4 | [[🧬 Biology]]
> This chapter focuses on birth control methods, medical termination of pregnancy, sexually transmitted diseases, and infertility treatments (ART).

---

## 🎯 Conceptual Core (NCERT Mirror)

### 1. Contraception Methods
- **Natural**: Periodic abstinence, Coitus interruptus, Lactational amenorrhea.
- **Barrier**: Condoms, Diaphragms.
- **IUDs (Intrauterine Devices)**:
  - Non-medicated: Lippes loop.
  - Copper releasing: CuT, Cu7, Multiload 375 (suppresses sperm motility).
  - Hormone releasing: Progestasert, LNG-20.
- **Oral Contraceptives**: "Saheli" (Non-steroidal, once-a-week pill).
- **Surgical**: Vasectomy (male), Tubectomy (female).

### 2. MTP & STDs
- **Medical Termination of Pregnancy (MTP)**: Legalized in India (1971). Safe up to 12 weeks.
- **STDs**: HIV, Hepatitis-B, Genital Herpes (Incurable). Chlamydia, Gonorrhea, Syphilis (Curable).

---

## 📐 The 12th Grade Technicals

### 🧪 Assisted Reproductive Technology (ART)
- **IVF (In-Vitro Fertilization)**:
  - **ZIFT**: Zygote Intra-Fallopian Transfer.
  - **IUT**: Intra Uterine Transfer.
- **GIFT**: Gamete Intra-Fallopian Transfer (for those who can't produce ovum but can provide environment).
- **ICSI**: Intra-Cytoplasmic Sperm Injection (sperm injected directly into ovum).
- **Artificial Insemination (AI/IUI)**: For low sperm count.

---

## ⚠️ The "Mistake DNA" Alert (Common Traps)
- **IUD Function**: Non-hormonal IUDs (CuT) affect **motility**, while hormonal ones (LNG-20) affect **ovulation/implantation**.
- **MTP Safety**: Safe till the 1st trimester (12 weeks); very risky in 2nd trimester.
- **Saheli**: Developed at CDRI, Lucknow.
- **Amniocentesis**: Used for chromosomal checks, but legally banned in India for **sex determination**.
