# 🌐 Ch08 - Electromagnetic Waves

> **Physics** | **Marks**: ~3 | [[🔬 Physics]]
> This chapter introduces the theoretical prediction of EM waves by Maxwell, the concept of Displacement Current, and the properties of the EM Spectrum.

---

## 🎯 Conceptual Core (NCERT Mirror)

### 1. Displacement Current ($I_D$)
- Maxwell predicted that a changing electric field induces a magnetic field. 
- **Definition**: $I_D = \epsilon_0 \frac{d\phi_E}{dt}$.
- **Ampere-Maxwell Law**: $\oint \vec{B} \cdot d\vec{l} = \mu_0 (I_C + I_D)$.

### 2. Properties of EM Waves
- Produced by **accelerated charges**.
- Transverse in nature: $\vec{E}, \vec{B},$ and direction of propagation are mutually perpendicular.
- Velocity in vacuum: $c = \frac{1}{\sqrt{\mu_0 \epsilon_0}} \approx 3 \times 10^8 m/s$.
- Ratio of magnitudes: $E_0 / B_0 = c$.

## 📐 The EM Spectrum (High-Yield)

| Wave Type | Frequency Range (approx) | Production | Application |
|-----------|-------------------------|------------|-------------|
| **Radio Waves** | $> 0.1 m$ | Rapid acceleration of electrons | Radio/TV communication |
| **Microwaves** | $0.1 m - 1 mm$ | Klystron/Magnetron valves | Radar, Microwave ovens |
| **Infrared** | $1 mm - 700 nm$ | Vibration of atoms/molecules | Remote controls, Green house effect |
| **Visible** | $700 nm - 400 nm$ | Electrons in atoms | Sight |
| **Ultraviolet** | $400 nm - 1 nm$ | Inner shell electrons | Sterilization, Water purifiers |
| **X-Rays** | $1 nm - 10^{-3} nm$ | Bombarding metal target | Medical imaging, Security |
| **Gamma Rays** | $< 10^{-3} nm$ | Radioactive decay | Cancer treatment |

## ⚠️ The "Mistake DNA" Alert (Common Traps)
- **Medium Speed**: In a medium, $v = \frac{1}{\sqrt{\mu \epsilon}} = \frac{c}{n}$. Don't use $c$ for glass/water.
- **Spectrum Order**: Forgetting the order of frequency or wavelength. Use mnemonics like: **R**ich **M**en **I**n **V**egas **U**se **X**-ray **G**lasses.
- **Transverse proof**: EM waves don't require a medium for propagation.
