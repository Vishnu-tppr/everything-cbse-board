# 📐 Master Formula Sheet (CBSE 12th)

> Every numerical without a formula and unit is a lost 0.5 - 1 mark. Use this sheet for daily retrieval practice.

---

## 📘 Mathematics (High-Weightage)

### 📈 Calculus
- **Differentiation**: $\frac{d}{dx}(a^x) = a^x \log a$ ; $\frac{d}{dx}(\sin^{-1} x) = \frac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^2}}$.
- **Integration**: $\int \frac{1}{\sqrt{a^2-x^2}} dx = \sin^{-1} \frac{x}{a} + C$.
- **Integration by Parts**: $\int uv dx = u \int v dx - \int [u' \int v dx] dx$.
- **Definite Integral ($P_4$)**: $\int_0^a f(x)dx = \int_0^a f(a-x)dx$.

### 📐 3D Geometry & Vectors
- **Shortest Distance ($SD$)**: $d = \left| \frac{(\vec{a}_2 - \vec{a}_1) \cdot (\vec{b}_1 \times \vec{b}_2)}{|\vec{b}_1 \times \vec{b}_2|} \right|$.
- **Angle between lines**: $\cos \theta = \frac{|\vec{b}_1 \cdot \vec{b}_2|}{|\vec{b}_1||\vec{b}_2|}$.

### 📊 Probability
- **Bayes' Theorem**: $P(E_i|A) = \frac{P(E_i)P(A|E_i)}{\sum P(E_j)P(A|E_j)}$.

## 🔬 Physics (The 70-Mark Base)

### ⚡ Electrostatics & Current
- **Gauss Law**: $\oint \vec{E} \cdot d\vec{A} = \frac{q_{enclosed}}{\epsilon_0}$.
- **Capacitance**: $C = \frac{\kappa \epsilon_0 A}{d}$ (with dielectric slab).
- **Drift Velocity**: $v_d = \frac{eE\tau}{m} = \frac{I}{neA}$.
- **Kirchhoff's Loop Rule**: $\sum \Delta V = 0$.

### 🧲 Magnetism & AC
- **Ampere’s Law**: $\oint \vec{B} \cdot d\vec{l} = \mu_0 I_{enclosed}$.
- **Resonance Frequency**: $f_r = \frac{1}{2\pi \sqrt{LC}}$.
- **Transformer**: $\frac{V_s}{V_p} = \frac{N_s}{N_p} = \frac{I_p}{I_s}$.

### 🔭 Optics
- **Lens Maker's Formula**: $\frac{1}{f} = (n_{21} - 1) \left( \frac{1}{R_1} - \frac{1}{R_2} \right)$.
- **Magnification (Microscope)**: $M = \frac{L}{f_o} \left( 1 + \frac{D}{f_e} \right)$.

## 🧪 Chemistry (Physical Constants)

### 💧 Solutions
- **Relative Lowering of Vapour Pressure**: $\frac{P^o - P}{P^o} = i \chi_{solute}$.
- **Osmotic Pressure**: $\pi = i CRT$ (Always use $i$ for electrolytes).

### 🔋 Electrochemistry
- **Nernst Equation**: $E_{cell} = E^o_{cell} - \frac{0.0591}{n} \log Q$ (at $298K$).
- **Molar Conductivity**: $\Lambda_m = \frac{\kappa \times 1000}{M}$ (Unit: $S \cdot cm^2 \cdot mol^{-1}$).

### 🧪 Chemical Kinetics
- **First Order Rate**: $k = \frac{2.303}{t} \log \frac{[R]_0}{[R]}$.
- **Arrhenius Equation**: $\log \frac{k_2}{k_1} = \frac{E_a}{2.303R} \left( \frac{T_2 - T_1}{T_1 T_2} \right)$.
