# ⚡ Ch02 - Electrochemistry

> **Chemistry** | **Marks**: ~9 | [[🧪 Chemistry]]
> This chapter covers the transformation of chemical energy into electrical energy (Galvanic cells) and vice versa (Electrolytic cells).

---

## 🎯 Conceptual Core (NCERT Mirror)

### 1. Galvanic Cells
- **Anode**: Oxidation (-ve electrode).
- **Cathode**: Reduction (+ve electrode).
- **Cell Notation**: $Zn(s) | Zn^{2+}(aq) || Cu^{2+}(aq) | Cu(s)$.
- **Electrode Potential**: $E_{cell} = E_{cathode} - E_{anode}$.

### 2. Nernst Equation (Critical)
- $E_{cell} = E^\circ_{cell} - \frac{0.059}{n} \log Q$ (at $298 K$).
- **Equilibrium Constant**: $E^\circ_{cell} = \frac{0.059}{n} \log K_c$.
- **Gibbs Free Energy**: $\Delta G^\circ = -n F E^\circ_{cell}$.

---

## 📐 The 12th Grade Technicals

### 🧪 Conductance & Electrolysis
- **Resistance ($R$) & Resistivity ($\rho$)**: $R = \rho (l/A)$.
- **Conductivity ($\kappa$)**: $\kappa = \frac{1}{\rho} = G (l/A)$. (Unit: $S m^{-1}$).
- **Molar Conductivity ($\Lambda_m$)**: $\Lambda_m = \frac{\kappa \times 1000}{M}$.
- **Kohlrausch’s Law**: $\Lambda_m^\circ = \nu_+ \lambda_+^\circ + \nu_- \lambda_-^\circ$. (Used for weak electrolytes).

### 🔋 Batteries & Corrosion
- **Primary Cells**: Dry cell, Mercury cell.
- **Secondary Cells**: Lead-storage battery, Ni-Cd cell.
- **Fuel Cells**: $H_2 - O_2$ fuel cell.
- **Corrosion**: Electrochemical phenomenon. Anode: $Fe \to Fe^{2+} + 2e^-$. Prevention: Galvanization, Sacrificial protection.

---

## ⚠️ The "Mistake DNA" Alert (Common Traps)
- **Sign convention**: $\Delta G$ is negative for spontaneous reactions; $E_{cell}$ is positive.
- **Stoichiometry in Nernst**: $Q$ involves powers based on coefficients. If $n$ is 2, ensure all species are adjusted.
- **$\kappa$ vs $\Lambda_m$ with Dilution**: 
  - On dilution, **Conductivity ($\kappa$) decreases** (less ions per unit vol).
  - **Molar Conductivity ($\Lambda_m$) increases** (volume effect outweighs $\kappa$ decrease).
