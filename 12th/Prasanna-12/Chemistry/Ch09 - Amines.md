# 🧬 Ch09 - Amines

> **Chemistry** | **Marks**: ~6 | [[🧪 Chemistry]]
> This chapter covers organic derivatives of ammonia (Amines) and the highly versatile Diazonium Salts.

---

## 🎯 Conceptual Core (NCERT Mirror)

### 1. Basicity of Amines (Critical)
- Amines are basic due to lone pair on Nitrogen.
- **Gas Phase**: $3^\circ > 2^\circ > 1^\circ > NH_3$.
- **Aqueous Phase (Methyl substituted)**: $2^\circ > 1^\circ > 3^\circ > NH_3$.
- **Aqueous Phase (Ethyl substituted)**: $2^\circ > 3^\circ > 1^\circ > NH_3$.
- **Aniline**: Less basic than alkyl amines due to resonance (lone pair delocalized).

### 2. Preparation Methods
- **Gabriel Phthalimide Synthesis**: For $1^\circ$ aliphatic amines only.
- **Hoffmann Bromamide Degradation**: $RCONH_2 \xrightarrow{Br_2/NaOH} RNH_2$ (Amine with one less carbon).

---

## 📐 The 12th Grade Technicals

### 🧪 Chemical Tests
- **Carbylamine Reaction**: $1^\circ$ amines + $CHCl_3/KOH \to$ Foul smelling isocyanide.
- **Hinsberg Test**: Benzene sulphonyl chloride ($C_6H_5SO_2Cl$).
  - $1^\circ \to$ Soluble in alkali.
  - $2^\circ \to$ Insoluble in alkali.
  - $3^\circ \to$ No reaction.

### ⚡ Diazonium Salts
- **Preparation**: Aniline $\xrightarrow{NaNO_2/HCl, 0-5^\circ C}$ Benzene diazonium chloride.
- **Reactions**: 
  - **Sandmeyer**: Using $CuCl/CuBr/CuCN$ to replace $N_2^+$.
  - **Gatterman**: Using $Cu/HCl$ or $Cu/HBr$.
  - **Coupling Reactions**: Formation of Azodyes (Orange with phenol, Yellow with aniline).

---

## ⚠️ The "Mistake DNA" Alert (Common Traps)
- **Basicity Anomalies**: In water, hydration and steric effects compete with the $+I$ effect. Don't just follow the $+I$ trend.
- **Nitration of Aniline**: In acidic medium, aniline is protonated to anilinium ion, which is **meta-directing**, leading to significant meta-nitroaniline.
- **Gabriel Constraint**: Aryl halides cannot be used as they do not undergo $S_N$ with phthalimide.
