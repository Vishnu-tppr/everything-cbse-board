# ⏱️ Ch03 - Chemical Kinetics

> **Chemistry** | **Marks**: ~7 | [[🧪 Chemistry]]
> This chapter analyzes the rate of chemical reactions, the factors affecting them, and the mechanisms of reaction.

---

## 🎯 Conceptual Core (NCERT Mirror)

### 1. Rate of Reaction
- **Average Rate**: $\Delta [R] / \Delta t$.
- **Instantaneous Rate**: $-d[R]/dt = +d[P]/dt$.

### 2. Rate Law & Order
- **Rate Law**: $Rate = k [A]^x [B]^y$.
- **Order of Reaction**: $n = x + y$. (Experimental value; can be $0, 1, 2,$ or fractional).
- **Molecularity**: Number of reacting species in an elementary step. (Theoretical; only integers $1, 2, 3$).

### 3. Integrated Rate Equations
- **Zero Order**: $[R] = [R]_0 - kt$. Half life $t_{1/2} = [R]_0 / 2k$.
- **First Order**: $k = \frac{2.303}{t} \log \frac{[R]_0}{[R]}$. Half life $t_{1/2} = 0.693 / k$.
- **Pseudo First Order**: Reactions that are higher order but behave as first order (e.g., Hydrolysis of esters in excess water).

---

## 📐 The 12th Grade Technicals

### 🧪 Temperature Dependence
- **Arrhenius Equation**: $k = A e^{-E_a/RT}$.
- **Linear form**: $\log \frac{k_2}{k_1} = \frac{E_a}{2.303 R} \left( \frac{1}{T_1} - \frac{1}{T_2} \right)$.
- **Activation Energy** ($E_a$): Minimum energy required for reactants to form the activated complex.

### 💥 Collision Theory
- Reactions occur when molecules collide with proper **Orientation** and **Threshold Energy**.
- Rate = $Z_{AB} e^{-E_a/RT}$.

---

## ⚠️ The "Mistake DNA" Alert (Common Traps)
- **Order vs Molecularity**: Order is experimental and applies to the overall reaction; Molecularity applies only to elementary steps.
- **Half-life independence**: For First Order, $t_{1/2}$ is **independent** of initial concentration. For Zero Order, it is **directly proportional**.
- **Units of $k$**: 
  - Zero: $mol \, L^{-1} s^{-1}$
  - First: $s^{-1}$
  - Second: $L \, mol^{-1} s^{-1}$
