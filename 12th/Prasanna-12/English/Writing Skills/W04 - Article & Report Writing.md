# 📝 W04 - Article & Report Writing

> **English** | **Marks**: 5 | [[📖 English]]
> Long-form writing requiring logical organization of ideas, good vocabulary, and a formal tone.

---

## 🎯 Article Writing (Format)
1. **Title/Heading**: Eye-catching and relevant.
2. **By-line**: Name of the writer.
3. **Introduction**: Define the topic and hook the reader.
4. **Analysis/Body**: 
   - Paragraph 1: Causes/Current state.
   - Paragraph 2: Consequences/Effects.
5. **Conclusion**: Solutions, suggestions, or a concluding thought.

---

## 📐 Report Writing (Format)
1. **Heading**: Describing the event.
2. **By-line**: Name of the reporter.
3. **Date & Place**: (e.g., 22 April, Delhi).
4. **Body**: 
   - **Lead Paragraph**: Who, what, when, where, why.
   - **Details**: Sequential account of the event.
   - **Conclusion**: Final outcome or comment.

---

## ⚠️ The "Mistake DNA" Alert (Common Traps)
- **Tense for Reports**: Always use **Past Tense** (since the event has already happened).
- **Objectivity in Reports**: Don't use "I"; stick to factual reporting.
- **Article Structure**: Use at least 3-4 paragraphs. Don't write one long chunk of text.
- **Heading**: Capitalize the first letter of each word in the heading.
