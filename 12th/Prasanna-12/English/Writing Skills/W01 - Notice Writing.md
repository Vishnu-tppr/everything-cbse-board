# 📝 W01 - Notice Writing

> **English** | **Marks**: 4 | [[📖 English]]
> A notice is a formal means of communication used to announce events, occasions, or provide information to a specific group.

---

## 🎯 The Formal Format (NCERT Standard)

1. **Name of Issuing Organization/School** (In Center)
2. **NOTICE** (In Capital letters, Center)
3. **Date of Issue** (Left side: 22 April 2026)
4. **Heading/Subject** (Catchy and relevant, Center)
5. **Body** (Content: Who, what, when, where, why)
6. **Name and Designation** of the issuer (Left side)

> **⚠️ BOX**: A notice must ALWAYS be enclosed in a box.

---

## 📐 The 12th Grade Technicals

### 🧪 Body Requirements (The 5 W's)
- **What**: The event (Meeting, tour, competition).
- **When**: Date and Time.
- **Where**: Venue.
- **Who**: Target audience.
- **Whom**: Contact person for further details.

### ⚡ Language and Style
- Formal and objective.
- Third-person perspective (Avoid "I" and "You").
- Word limit: **50 words**.

---

## ⚠️ The "Mistake DNA" Alert (Common Traps)
- **Word Limit**: Do not exceed 50 words; marks are deducted for wordiness.
- **The Box**: Forgetting to draw a box around the notice.
- **Date Format**: Use "22 April 2026" or "April 22, 2026". Avoid "22/04/26".
- **Heading**: Make sure the heading is not too long.
