# 📝 W03 - Job Application with Biodata

> **English** | **Marks**: 5 | [[📖 English]]
> A job application consists of two parts: a **Covering Letter** and a **Bio-data/Resumé**.

---

## 🎯 Part 1: The Covering Letter (Format)
1. **Sender's Address**
2. **Date**
3. **Receiver's Designation & Address** (e.g., The Manager, XYZ Co.)
4. **Subject**: Application for the post of [Post Name]
5. **Salutation**: Sir/Madam
6. **Body**:
   - **Opening**: Reference to the advertisement (e.g., "This is in response to your advertisement in 'The Hindu' dated...")
   - **Offer**: Stating suitability for the post.
   - **Conclusion**: Referring to the enclosed bio-data.
7. **Complimentary Close**: Yours faithfully/sincerely
8. **Name**

---

## 📐 Part 2: The Bio-data (Components)
- **Personal Details**: Name, Father’s Name, DOB, Address, Contact No.
- **Educational Qualification**: (Use a table: Exam, Board/Uni, Year, Percentage).
- **Professional Experience**: List previous jobs.
- **Skills**: Languages known, computer skills.
- **References**: Names and contacts of two prominent people.

---

## ⚠️ The "Mistake DNA" Alert (Common Traps)
- **Table for Qualifications**: Always use a table for education; it looks professional and scores better.
- **Reference to Ad**: Forgetting to mention the newspaper and the date of the advertisement.
- **Sign-off**: Use "Yours faithfully" (if recipient name not known) or "Yours sincerely" (if known). Note: "Yours" has **no apostrophe**.
- **Biodata Box**: It is recommended to put the Biodata in a box.
