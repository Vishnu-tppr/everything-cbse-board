# 📖 V02 - The Tiger King

> **English** | **Author**: Kalki | [[📖 English]]
> A satirical story about the arrogance of those in power, centered around the Maharaja of Pratibandapuram and his quest to kill 100 tigers.

---

## 🎯 Conceptual Core (NCERT Mirror)

### 1. The Prophecy
- The astrologers predicted that the Maharaja would be killed by a tiger, specifically the hundredth one.
- **The Miracle**: The 10-day-old prince spoke and questioned the astrologers about the "manner" of his death.

### 2. The Quest
- The Maharaja killed tigers one by one, even banning tiger hunting for anyone else.
- He married a girl from a state with a high tiger population to reach his target of 100.

---

## 📐 The 12th Grade Technicals

### 🧪 Theme: Irony of Fate and Power
- **The 100th Tiger**: The Maharaja thought he killed it, but he missed. His hunters killed it to save their jobs.
- **The Death**: The "Tiger King" was finally killed by a **wooden toy tiger** that he bought for his son. A sliver of wood caused an infection.

### ⚡ Satire
- The way the Maharaja neglected his duties for his obsession.
- The behavior of his courtiers (the Dewan) who were too afraid to tell him the truth.

---

## ⚠️ The "Mistake DNA" Alert (Common Traps)
- **The British Officer**: The Maharaja almost lost his kingdom because he refused to let a British officer hunt. he saved it by sending 50 diamond rings to the officer's wife.
- **The Hunters' Secret**: The hunters knew the 100th tiger was only fainted, not dead, but they didn't tell the king out of fear.
- **The Final Tiger**: Fate is inescapable. Despite killing 99 real tigers, a "crude" wooden one proved fatal.
