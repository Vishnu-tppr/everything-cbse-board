# 🏆 Topper Answer Patterns — {{subject}}

> **Goal**: Reverse-engineer the CBSE marking scheme to structure answers for maximum score.

---

## 📋 The Anatomy of a 5-Mark Answer
1. **Given Data**: Clearly listed with units.
2. **Formula/Logic**: Written in a separate box.
3. **Step-by-Step Solving**: No skipping logical jumps.
4. **Final Answer**: Boxed with correct SI unit.
5. **Diagram/Graph**: Labeled, framed, and referred to in text.

---

## 📉 Marking Scheme vs. Topper Copy
| Question Type | What the Board Wants | Topper's Hack |
|---------------|----------------------|---------------|
| **1M** | Direct value point | One concise line + keyword |
| **3M** | 3 clear logical steps | Use bullet points (NCERT style) |
| **5M** | Comprehensive logic | Diagram + Derivation + Case Study |

---

## ⚠️ Mark-Deduction Traps (Avoid These)
- [ ] **Ambiguity**: Using "it" instead of the specific noun.
- [ ] **Unit Error**: Writing 10 instead of 10 Tesla/m².
- [ ] **Graph Precision**: Not labeling axes or scale.
