# 📐 Ch10 - Vector Algebra

> **Mathematics** | **Marks**: ~6-7 | [[📘 Mathematics]]
> This chapter introduces vectors as directed line segments, covering operations like Addition, Scalar Multiplication, and Dot/Cross products.

---

## 🎯 Conceptual Core (NCERT Mirror)

### 1. Basics
- **Magnitude**: $|\vec{a}| = \sqrt{x^2 + y^2 + z^2}$.
- **Unit Vector**: $\hat{a} = \frac{\vec{a}}{|\vec{a}|}$.
- **Direction Cosines ($l, m, n$)**: $l = \cos \alpha, m = \cos \beta, n = \cos \gamma$. Note: $l^2 + m^2 + n^2 = 1$.
- **Direction Ratios ($a, b, c$)**: Proportional to direction cosines.

### 2. Operations
- **Dot Product (Scalar)**: $\vec{a} \cdot \vec{b} = |\vec{a}| |\vec{b}| \cos \theta$.
  - If $\vec{a} \perp \vec{b}$, then $\vec{a} \cdot \vec{b} = 0$.
- **Cross Product (Vector)**: $\vec{a} \times \vec{b} = |\vec{a}| |\vec{b}| \sin \theta \, \hat{n}$.
  - If $\vec{a} \parallel \vec{b}$, then $\vec{a} \times \vec{b} = \vec{0}$.

---

## 📐 The 12th Grade Technicals

### 🧪 Projections & Area
- **Projection of $\vec{a}$ on $\vec{b}$**: $\frac{\vec{a} \cdot \vec{b}}{|\vec{b}|}$.
- **Area of Parallelogram**: $|\vec{a} \times \vec{b}|$ (with $\vec{a}, \vec{b}$ as adjacent sides).
- **Area of Triangle**: $\frac{1}{2} |\vec{a} \times \vec{b}|$.

### ⚡ Determinant Form (Cross Product)
$\vec{a} \times \vec{b} = \begin{vmatrix} \hat{i} & \hat{j} & \hat{k} \\ a_1 & a_2 & a_3 \\ b_1 & b_2 & b_3 \end{vmatrix}$

---

## ⚠️ The "Mistake DNA" Alert (Common Traps)
- **Dot vs Cross result**: Dot product is a **number**; Cross product is a **vector**.
- **Unit Vector vs Vector**: Ensure you divide by magnitude when asked for a unit vector.
- **Triangle inequality**: $|\vec{a} + \vec{b}| \leq |\vec{a}| + |\vec{b}|$.
