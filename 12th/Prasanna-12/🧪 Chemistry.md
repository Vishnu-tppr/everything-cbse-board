## 🚀 The Organic Giant (33 Marks)

Organic Chemistry is the make-or-break section. Focus on:
- **Named Reactions**: Reimer-Tiemann, Kolbe's, Aldol, Cannizzaro, Hoffmann Bromamide.
- **Mechanisms**: SN1 vs SN2, Dehydration of Ethanol, Nucleophilic Addition.
- **Distinction Tests**: Lucas Test, Iodoform, Tollens', Hinsberg's.
- **Conversions**: $A \to B \to C$ roadmaps using the `/organic-roadmap` command.

---

## ⚡ Physical Chemistry mastery (23 Marks)
- **Solutions**: Raoult's Law & Colligative Properties (van't Hoff factor).
- **Electro**: Nernst Equation (5M guaranteed numerical) & Kohlrausch's Law.
- **Kinetics**: 1st Order Integrated Rate Law & Arrhenius Equation.
