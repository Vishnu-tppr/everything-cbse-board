## 🚀 The Genetics Giant (20 Marks)

Genetics is the intellectual core of 12th Bio. Focus on:
- **Principles of Inheritance**: Mendel's Laws, Sex Determination, Pedigree Analysis.
- **Molecular Basis**: DNA Replication, Transcription, Translation, Lac Operon.
- **Evolution**: Adaptive Radiation, Hardy-Weinberg Principle.
