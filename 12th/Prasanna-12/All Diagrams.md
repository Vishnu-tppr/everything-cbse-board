# 🎨 Master Diagram Bank (CBSE 12th)

> Diagrams carry **15-20 marks** in Biology and **10-12 marks** in Physics. A diagram without labels is worth zero. Always use a pencil and label on the right side.

---

## 🧬 Biology (High-Weightage NCERT)
- **Unit VI: Reproduction**
  - T.S. of Anther & Structure of Microsporangium.
  - Embryo Sac (7-celled, 8-nucleate).
  - Human Sperm & Ovum structure.
- **Unit VII: Genetics**
  - Nucleosome model.
  - Replication Fork & Transcription Unit.
  - Lac Operon (Switch ON/OFF states).
- **Unit IX: Biotechnology**
  - pBR322 Plasmid Vector (Mandatory labeling: $ori, amp^R, tet^R$).
  - Simple Stirred-tank Bioreactor.
  - PCR Steps (Denaturation, Annealing, Extension).

---

## 🔭 Physics (Mandatory Ray & Circuit)
- **Unit I & II: Electrostatics & Current**
  - Electric Field lines for Dipole & Like charges.
  - Parallel Plate Capacitor (with/without Dielectric).
  - Meter Bridge & Potentiometer circuits.
- **Unit III & IV: Magnetism & AC**
  - Moving Coil Galvanometer (Schematic).
  - Cyclotron (Schematic).
  - AC Generator & Transformer (Core/Coils).
- **Unit VI: Optics**
  - **Ray Optics**: Compound Microscope & Astronomical Telescope (Normal adjustment).
  - **Wave Optics**: Wavefront (Plane/Spherical) & YDSE (Experimental setup).
- **Unit IX: Devices**
  - p-n Junction (Forward vs Reverse Bias characteristics).
  - Full-wave Rectifier circuit & Input/Output waveforms.

---

## 🧪 Chemistry (Structural & Electrochemical)
- **Unit II: Electrochemistry**
  - Galvanic Cell (Daniel Cell) with Salt Bridge.
  - Standard Hydrogen Electrode (SHE).
  - Fuel Cell ($H_2-O_2$ type).
- **Unit V: Coordination Compounds**
  - Geometrical Isomers (cis/trans) for $[Co(NH_3)_4Cl_2]^+$.
  - Splitting of d-orbitals in Octahedral vs Tetrahedral fields (CFT).
- **Unit X: Biomolecules**
  - Structure of $\alpha$-D-Glucose & $\beta$-D-Glucose.
  - Peptide linkage formation.
  - Watson-Crick model of DNA.

---

## 💻 Computer Science (Layouts & Logic)
- **Unit II: Networking**
  - Network Topologies (Bus, Star, Tree).
  - Layout designing for Case Studies (School/Office).

---

> [!TIP]
> **Checklist for Full Marks**:
> 1. Use a sharp HB pencil.
> 2. Labeling lines should not cross each other.
> 3. Give a clear **Title** below the diagram (e.g., "Fig 1: Reimer-Tiemann Mechanism").
